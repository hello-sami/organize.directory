About The Directory

The Organize Directory is a resource for finding and connecting with mutual aid networks and grassroots initiatives across different cities and focus areas. We believe in the power of community support and solidarity.

Mutual aid is a form of community support where people work together to meet each other's needs, based on the principles of solidarity and reciprocity rather than charity.
