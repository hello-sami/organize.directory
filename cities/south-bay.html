Racial Justice

SURJ Santa Clara County

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing across Santa Clara County. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations from San Jose to Palo Alto, with a focus on decarceration and protecting indigenous land.
