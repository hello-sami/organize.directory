Hampton Roads Harm Reduction

A community-based harm reduction organization providing support, resources, and education to people who use drugs in the Hampton Roads area. They work to reduce drug-related harms through mutual aid, advocacy, and direct support services.

757 Bail Fund

A community bail fund serving the Hampton Roads area, working to end wealth-based detention through direct action and mutual aid. They provide bail assistance to community members who cannot afford it, while advocating for systemic change in the criminal justice system.

Hampton Roads Regional Jail Line

A mutual aid initiative providing support and resources to incarcerated individuals and their families in Hampton Roads. They work to maintain connections between inmates and their communities while advocating for better conditions and systemic change.
