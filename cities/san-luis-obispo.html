Food Not Bombs San Luis Obispo

FNB is an all-volunteer movement that recovers food that would otherwise be discarded & shares free meals with the hungry.

SLO Bangers

A harm reduction program providing syringe exchange services and overdose prevention to reduce the spread of blood borne pathogens and support those struggling with substance dependency in San Luis Obispo County. They operate multiple outreach locations throughout the week in Morro Bay, Paso Robles, and Atascadero.
