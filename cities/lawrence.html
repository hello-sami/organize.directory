LFK Mutual Aid

A grassroots network coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives in Lawrence, Kansas (LFK). They organize direct assistance, resource sharing, and neighborhood solidarity while building sustainable networks of care and support through community organizing and collective action.

ECM KU Solidarity Library

A community library fostering mutual aid and radical education through free book access and cultural programming in Lawrence. They coordinate book lending, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through accessible knowledge sharing, community dialogue, and creating spaces for learning and movement building.

Lawrence Food Not Bombs

Grassroots organization cooking free meals once a week.

Lawrence Tenants

Tenants organizing to make it easier to rent in Lawrence. We are currently organizing for right to counsel for tenants facing eviction

Trans Lawrence Coalition

Trans Lawrence Coalition is a trans-led organization seeking to protect & expand the social and political well-being of trans people in Lawrence, KS.
