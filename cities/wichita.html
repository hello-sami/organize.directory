Peace Connections

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through peace education and conflict resolution in Wichita. They coordinate nonviolence training, restorative justice programs, and solidarity networks while building power through community dialogue, youth empowerment, and creating spaces for peaceful conflict transformation.

Safe Streets Wichita

Safe Streets Wichita, Inc. advances public health prevention by expanding access to naloxone, emergency contraception (EC), linkages to care, substance misuse prevention, and other life-saving tools.
