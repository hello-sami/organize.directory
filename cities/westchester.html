Westchester, New York

Local Organizations

WESPAC Foundation

A leading force for progressive social change in Westchester County since 1974, WESPAC has been educating, agitating and organizing for a more just and peaceful world, an end to militarism and racism and a more fair economy that works for all. They coordinate community organizing, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through grassroots activism and creating spaces for social justice.

SURJ Westchester

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Westchester County. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
