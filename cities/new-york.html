We Build The Block

A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and community-led public safety initiatives in New York City. They organize youth leadership development, restorative justice programs, and innovative safety protocols while building networks of solidarity through community education, trauma resolution, and transformative approaches to neighborhood wellbeing.

Parole Preparation Project

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through advocacy, litigation, and direct support for currently and formerly incarcerated New Yorkers. They coordinate parole preparation assistance, community care programs, and solidarity networks while building power through policy advocacy, arts programming, and creating spaces for successful reentry and community reintegration.
