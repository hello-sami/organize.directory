Mutual Aid Networks

Bronx Mutual Aid Network

A grassroots collective providing direct support and community care in the Bronx. They coordinate resource distribution, emergency assistance, and solidarity networks while building power through neighborhood-based organizing and mutual aid initiatives.

The BLK Mutual Aid

A community-led mutual aid initiative serving the Bronx through food distribution, resource sharing, and community support programs.

South Bronx Mutual Aid

South Bronx Mutual Aid is a volunteer group of neighbors helping neighbors.

Food Support

Friendly Fridge BX

A mutual aid collective coordinating community fridges and food distribution initiatives in the Bronx. They organize direct support through free food access, resource sharing, and community education while building networks of solidarity through neighborhood organizing and food justice advocacy.

NY Common Pantry

Reducing hunger and promoting dignity, health, and self-sufficiency with locations in East Harlem and the Bronx.

Community Organizing

Take Back The Bronx

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and community power through direct action and organizing in The Bronx. They coordinate tenant organizing, anti-gentrification initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through community defense, political education, and creating spaces for collective resistance against displacement and police violence.
