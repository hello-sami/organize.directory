Champaign-Urbana

7 groups · Illinois

  1. SURJ Champaign-Urbana ↗ facebook.com

    A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in the Champaign-Urbana area. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

  2. Free Champaign Urbana ↗ instagram.com

    CU mutual aid group primarily focused on resource sharing, monthly food distributions, and community building. Free resources, free people.

  3. Praire Liberation Center ↗ plcweb.org

    Working-class community center based out of Champaign, IL

  4. Urbana Champaign Forest Defense ↗ instagram.com

    Organizing to Stop Cop City and Defend the Atlanta Forest from the Champaign Urbana area.

  5. Urbana Champaign Reproductive Justice ↗ ucrj.org

    Dedicated to lifting up and solidifying the Urbana-Champaign area's commitment to achieving reproductive justice in all its forms for our community.

  6. UC Books to Prisoners ↗ books2prisoners.org

    An Urbana Illinois based project providing books to incarcerated individuals in Illinois at no cost by mail as well as through the county jail library which we operate.

  7. UC Jews For Ceasefire ↗ instagram.com

    We are a multigenerational group of Jews in the Urbana-Champaign area organizing for a permanent and immediate ceasefire in Gaza.
