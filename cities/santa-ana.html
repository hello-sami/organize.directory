Santa Ana

Tenants United Santa Ana

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through tenant organizing and housing justice. They coordinate direct support for tenants, eviction defense, and solidarity networks while building power through collective action, tenant rights education, and creating spaces for housing justice and community care.
