Burning Books

A radical bookstore coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives in Buffalo. They organize resource sharing, political education, and solidarity networks while building community power through radical literature, events, and collective organizing.

SURJ Buffalo

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Buffalo. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
