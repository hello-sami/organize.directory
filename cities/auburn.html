Auburn, AL

Auburn Students for Palestine

A student-led mutual aid and solidarity collective organizing support and resources for Palestine, while building community awareness and engagement at Auburn University.

Auburn DSA

A local chapter of the Democratic Socialists of America organizing mutual aid initiatives and community support networks in the Auburn area. They coordinate various mutual aid projects through their social media channels and community partnerships.

Lee County Remembrance Project

A community coalition working to memorialize documented victims of racial terror lynchings in Lee County through research, education, and community organizing. They coordinate mutual aid and community support initiatives focused on racial justice and historical truth-telling.

Lee County Literacy Coalition

A community organization providing free literacy services through mutual aid and volunteer networks. They offer one-to-one tutoring, family literacy programs, ESL services, and digital, workplace, financial, and health literacy education to empower individuals in the Lee County community.
