Freeride Montpelier

A collectively organized non-profit bike shop providing mutual aid through pay-what-you-can pricing and community access to bicycles, parts, and repair knowledge. They promote sustainable transportation while ensuring no one is turned away for lack of funds, offering DIY repair spaces, education, and sliding-scale pricing on all services to make cycling accessible to everyone in the community.
