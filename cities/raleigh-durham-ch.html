Feed Durham NC

A grassroots mutual aid collective organizing food distribution and community support in Durham. They coordinate food rescue, resource sharing, and direct support while building sustainable networks of food access and community care.

Durham Beyond Policing

A grassroots coalition organizing mutual aid and community support while building towards a Durham that invests in people, not police. They coordinate resource sharing, direct support initiatives, and community organizing while advocating for transformative justice and community-led safety solutions.

Pauli Murray Center for History & Social Justice

A historic site and community center dedicated to advancing social justice through preserving the legacy of Rev. Dr. Pauli Murray, a groundbreaking civil rights activist, legal scholar, feminist, author, and Episcopal priest. They coordinate educational programs, community dialogues, arts initiatives, and mutual aid organizing while connecting Durham's history of activism to contemporary human rights issues.

Rainbow Collective for Change

An organization building mutual aid networks and community support for LGBTQ+ families with children in Durham. They coordinate direct support through playgroups, educational programs, and family resources while building inclusive spaces and advocating for LGBTQ+ family equity through community organizing and solidarity networks.

Triangle Art for Aid

A mutual aid collective organizing art-based fundraising and community support in the Triangle area. They coordinate resource sharing and direct support through art sales and creative initiatives while building networks of solidarity between artists and community needs.

Durham Prison Letters

A mutual aid collective providing support and connection to incarcerated individuals in Durham through letter writing and resource sharing. They coordinate correspondence, educational materials, and solidarity networks while working to break isolation and build connections between communities inside and outside prison walls.

Comrades in the Commons

A mutual aid collective organizing community support and resource sharing in Durham. They coordinate direct support initiatives, solidarity networks, and community education while building power through collective action and grassroots organizing.

People's Solidarity Hub

A mutual aid collective organizing community support and resource sharing in Durham. They coordinate direct support initiatives, solidarity networks, and community spaces while building sustainable networks of mutual aid through shared resources, education, and collective organizing.
