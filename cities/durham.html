The Regulator Bookshop

An independent bookstore fostering mutual aid through accessible literature and community gathering space. They support local authors, host educational events, and create spaces for community dialogue while serving as a hub for knowledge sharing and cultural exchange.

DataWorks NC

A community-based organization fostering mutual aid and data justice through participatory research and technology in Durham. They coordinate data analysis, community mapping, and solidarity networks while building power through accessible data tools, neighborhood histories, and creating spaces for data-driven advocacy against displacement and inequity.

Food Not Bombs Durham

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Durham. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.

Changed Paths Durham

A mutual aid project in East Durham.
