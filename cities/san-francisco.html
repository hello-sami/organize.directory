Housing Justice

San Francisco Tenants Union

Member-supported organization fighting for tenants' rights and housing justice through direct support, education, and collective organizing.

Coalition on Homelessness

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through homeless advocacy and organizing in San Francisco. They coordinate direct support, policy advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through homeless-led organizing, street outreach, and creating spaces for housing justice and systemic change.

Eviction Defense Collaborative

A legal services organization fostering mutual aid and housing justice through eviction defense and tenant support in San Francisco. They coordinate legal assistance, rental assistance, and solidarity networks while building power through direct representation, tenant counseling, and creating spaces for housing justice advocacy.

SF Anti-Displacement Coalition

A coalition of community organizations fostering mutual aid and housing justice through collective organizing against displacement in San Francisco. They coordinate tenant organizing, policy advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through community education, direct action, and creating spaces for housing justice organizing.

Causa Justa :: Just Cause (CJJC)

Multi-racial, grassroots organization building community power through tenant rights advocacy, mutual aid, and organizing against gentrification and displacement in San Francisco and Oakland.

Community Organizations

PODER SF

A grassroots environmental and economic justice organization building power in San Francisco's Latinx immigrant communities through mutual aid, participatory action research, and organizing for environmental justice, housing rights, and community self-determination.

Chinese Progressive Association

Grassroots organization building collective power with working-class Chinese immigrants through mutual aid, worker and tenant organizing, youth leadership development, and environmental justice initiatives.

People Power Media

An independent media organization fostering mutual aid and community power through grassroots journalism and media education in San Francisco. They coordinate community reporting, media training, and solidarity networks while building power through accessible journalism, movement storytelling, and creating spaces for community-driven narratives.

SF Mutual Aid

A community network coordinating mutual aid efforts and connecting neighbors to share resources across San Francisco through direct support, solidarity, and collective care.

GLIDE

A nationally recognized center for social justice fostering mutual aid and community care in the Tenderloin since 1963. They coordinate comprehensive direct support services including free meals, housing assistance, and health services while building power through advocacy initiatives, inclusive community building, and creating pathways out of poverty through collective organizing and solidarity networks.

The Women's Building

A women-led community space and mutual aid hub providing direct support, resource sharing, and solidarity networks. They coordinate community programs, educational initiatives, and mutual aid while building power through feminist organizing, cultural events, and creating spaces for collective care and movement building.

Resistance SF

General protest calendar and community organizing resource.

LGBTQ+ Resources

Queerious Labs

A queer-centered hackerspace and community workshop fostering mutual aid through technology education and skill-sharing. They provide tools, space, and learning opportunities while building networks of solidarity through DIY technology, collaborative projects, and creating accessible spaces for experimentation and collective empowerment.

The Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence

A leading-edge Order of queer and trans nuns fostering mutual aid and community care since 1979. They coordinate direct support initiatives, outreach programs, and solidarity networks while building power through humor, activism, and creating spaces for queer joy, human rights advocacy, and spiritual enlightenment. Their work includes supporting marginalized communities, promoting diversity, and organizing mutual aid events like SOCKSgiving for unhoused neighbors.

Lavender Phoenix

A grassroots organization building power for LGBTQ+ Asian & Pacific Islanders through mutual aid and community organizing. They coordinate direct support initiatives, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through intersectional organizing, cultural work, and creating spaces for queer and trans API leadership and collective liberation.

SF LGBT Center

A community center fostering mutual aid and solidarity through LGBTQ+ support services and community organizing. They coordinate direct assistance, youth programs, and economic empowerment initiatives while building power through community-led programs, cultural events, and creating spaces for queer and trans community care and collective empowerment.

Food Justice

San Francisco Food Not Bombs

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through free food distribution and anti-hunger organizing. They coordinate food recovery, community meals, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action against food waste and militarism, sharing vegan meals at 16th & Mission BART Plaza on Wednesdays and 65 9th St. on Fridays.

Arts & Culture

Silver Sprocket

An independent publisher and community space fostering mutual aid through socially conscious comic books, graphic novels, and arts. Their retail store and gallery in the Mission District (1018 Valencia St) hosts regular educational and community events while building networks of solidarity through independent artists and radical literature.

Betti Ono

A Black-led cultural organization fostering community power through art, culture, and mutual aid in the Bay Area. They coordinate exhibitions, artist support, and solidarity networks while building power through cultural organizing, community education, and creating spaces for artistic expression and social justice.
