Community Organizations

  1. PODER SF ↗ podersf.org

    A grassroots environmental and economic justice organization building power in San Francisco's Latinx immigrant communities through mutual aid, participatory action research, and organizing for environmental justice, housing rights, and community self-determination.

  2. Chinese Progressive Association ↗ cpasf.org

    Grassroots organization building collective power with working-class Chinese immigrants through mutual aid, worker and tenant organizing, youth leadership development, and environmental justice initiatives.

  3. People Power Media ↗ peoplepowermedia.org

    An independent media organization fostering mutual aid and community power through grassroots journalism and media education in San Francisco. They coordinate community reporting, media training, and solidarity networks while building power through accessible journalism, movement storytelling, and creating spaces for community-driven narratives.

  4. SF Mutual Aid ↗ sfmutualaid.com

    A community network coordinating mutual aid efforts and connecting neighbors to share resources across San Francisco through direct support, solidarity, and collective care.

  6. The Women's Building ↗ womensbuilding.org

    A women-led community space and mutual aid hub providing direct support, resource sharing, and solidarity networks.
