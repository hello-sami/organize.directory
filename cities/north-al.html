Land Trust of North Alabama

A community organization fostering mutual aid and environmental stewardship through land conservation and public access in North Alabama. They coordinate preservation of natural areas, maintain 80+ miles of public trails across 10 nature preserves, and build solidarity networks through 400+ volunteers while creating spaces for environmental education, community connection, and sustainable land management.
