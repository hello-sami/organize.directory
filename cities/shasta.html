Shasta

Mutual Aid Networks & Community Resources

Shasta Mutual Aid

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support in Shasta County. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective care.

UNHARM

A harm reduction and mutual aid collective serving the Shasta region. They provide direct support, resource distribution, and community care while building networks of solidarity through harm reduction practices and collective organizing.

Shasta for Palestine

Solidarity-driven, anti-colonial collective committed to the liberation of Palestine and all oppressed peoples.

Awareness into action. Solidarity not charity.
