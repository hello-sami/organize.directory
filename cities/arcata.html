Everything Is Free Now

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support through free markets and resource sharing in Arcata. They coordinate direct assistance, free item distribution, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for gift economy and collective care.

Food Not Bombs Arcata

We are a group of volunteers that recover and share free vegan food with the public to protest war, poverty, and the destruction of the environment. Meal distro is every Sunday at 4 at the Arcata Plaza.
