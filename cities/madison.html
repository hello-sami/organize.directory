Madison, Wisconsin

Community Organizing

Freedom, Inc.

A Black and Southeast Asian organization providing mutual aid, direct support, and community organizing to low-income communities of color in Madison. Through their intersectional approach, they work on gender justice, queer justice, Black and Southeast Asian liberation while providing culturally-specific services including domestic violence support, crisis intervention, housing assistance, economic advocacy, and safety planning. Their programming centers Black, Hmong, and Cambodian communities while building power through leadership development and community organizing.

Veterans for Peace Madison

A local chapter organizing mutual aid and community support initiatives while advocating for peace and justice. They coordinate resources and support for veterans and the broader Madison community through direct action and solidarity networks.

Mennonite Action Madison

A local chapter organizing mutual aid and solidarity actions for Palestine through faith-based community organizing. They coordinate resources, organize nonviolent direct actions, and build interfaith coalitions while providing material support and advocating for peace and justice.

Palestine Partners

A Madison-based organization providing mutual aid and support for human rights projects in Palestine, particularly the Women in Hebron craft cooperative and Youth of Sumud in the South Hebron Hills. They facilitate direct support and community connections between Madison and Palestinian communities.

Nehemiah Center

A Madison-based organization focused on empowering the African American community through education, economic development, and community support. They provide mutual aid through various programs including youth mentorship, reentry services, leadership development, and community organizing to address systemic inequities.

Catalyst for Change

A Madison-based nonprofit dedicated to supporting individuals experiencing homelessness through crisis management and wrap-around care services. They provide direct support and mutual aid while working to reimagine homeless services in Dane County through creative, community-based solutions.

Operation Fresh Start

A Madison-based organization empowering emerging adults through education, mentoring, and employment training. They provide mutual aid and support through various programs including construction training, conservation work, healthcare career preparation, and driver's education, while helping participants earn high school diplomas and develop career skills.

The Center for Black Excellence and Culture

A groundbreaking community-led initiative in Madison creating a dedicated space to cultivate and celebrate Black culture, excellence, and community power. Led by Black leaders and shaped by hundreds of Black voices, The Center provides a hub for cultural affirmation, community building, and collective advancement of Madison's Black community.

Trans Advocacy Madison

An independent grassroots advocacy group by and for the transgender, non-binary, Two-Spirit, and gender-expansive community in Madison. They organize direct action, mutual aid, and community support through protests, campaigns, and resource sharing, while building solidarity networks and providing educational resources.

World Beyond War Madison

A local chapter of the global nonviolent movement organizing for peace and justice through direct action and mutual aid. They coordinate community initiatives including peace vigils, antiwar film series, and advocacy campaigns while building solidarity networks to support demilitarization and diplomatic solutions to conflict.

OutReach LGBTQ+ Community Center

A long-standing community center providing comprehensive support and mutual aid services for the LGBTQ+ community in Madison for over 50 years. They offer various programs including harm reduction, housing services, elder support, social groups, transgender services, and educational resources while fostering community connections and providing direct assistance.

Justice for Kenosha's Disabled Population

A grassroots mutual aid organization advocating for and providing direct support to disabled community members. They organize community resources, coordinate support networks, and build solidarity through direct action and advocacy while working to ensure accessibility and justice for disabled populations.

Planned Parenthood Wisconsin Madison Supporters

A grassroots collective organizing mutual aid and community support for reproductive healthcare access in Madison. They coordinate solidarity networks, resource sharing, and direct support while advocating for reproductive justice and healthcare accessibility.

Social Justice Center Madison

A community hub organizing mutual aid and social justice initiatives in Madison. They provide space and resources for grassroots organizing, coordinate solidarity networks, and support direct action campaigns while building connections between various social justice movements and community groups.

Madison for Palestine

A grassroots collective organizing mutual aid and solidarity actions in support of Palestine. They coordinate community resources, direct support initiatives, and educational events while building connections between Madison and Palestinian communities through mutual aid networks and solidarity campaigns.

Madison IWW

The Madison branch of the Industrial Workers of the World, organizing mutual aid and worker solidarity through direct action and community support. They coordinate labor organizing, workplace democracy initiatives, and solidarity networks while building connections between various social movements and worker struggles.

Madison TRAC

A tenant resource and action center providing mutual aid and organizing support for renters in Madison. They coordinate tenant rights education, eviction defense, housing advocacy, and direct support while building solidarity networks to address housing justice issues and protect tenant rights.

Madison-Rafah Sister City Project

A solidarity organization building connections and providing mutual aid between Madison and Rafah, Gaza. They coordinate direct support initiatives, educational programs, and humanitarian aid while fostering community ties and organizing solidarity actions to support Palestinian families and communities.

Jewish Voice for Peace Madison

A grassroots organization of Jewish and allied activists working for justice, equality, and dignity for all in Palestine/Israel through education, community organizing, and mutual aid initiatives. They coordinate solidarity actions, resource sharing, and direct support while building connections between Madison's Jewish community and Palestinian solidarity movements.

Madison Socialist Alternative

A local chapter organizing mutual aid and community support initiatives while building working class power through direct action and solidarity networks. They coordinate resource sharing, political education, and community organizing while connecting various social movements and providing material support to communities in need.

Madison Community Fridges

A mutual aid network organizing free community fridges and food distribution across Madison. They coordinate food rescue, resource sharing, and direct support while building sustainable networks of food access and community care.

Madison Abortion & Reproductive Rights Coalition for Healthcare (MARRCH)

A grassroots coalition organizing mutual aid and community support for reproductive healthcare access and abortion rights in Madison. They coordinate direct action, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building community power through education, advocacy, and collective organizing.

CODEPINK Madison

A grassroots peace and social justice organization coordinating mutual aid and solidarity initiatives in Madison. They organize direct action, resource sharing, and community support while building networks of solidarity through anti-war organizing and peace advocacy.
