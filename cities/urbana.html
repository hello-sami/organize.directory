Urbana

Cultural Centers

Urbana-Champaign Independent Media Center

A grassroots cultural center fostering mutual aid and community power through independent media, arts, and organizing in Urbana-Champaign. They coordinate community radio, a zine library, books to prisoners program, makerspace, and solidarity networks while building power through radical education, cultural programming, and creating accessible spaces for movement building and social change.

Prison Support

First Followers

A peer-led organization fostering mutual aid and community power through reentry support and prison abolition work in Urbana-Champaign. They coordinate direct assistance, mentoring, and solidarity networks while building power through peer support, leadership development, and creating pathways for formerly incarcerated people to rebuild their lives.

Food Security

Feeding Our Kids

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through food security initiatives for children in Champaign County. They coordinate weekend food programs, resource distribution, and solidarity networks while building power through direct support, community partnerships, and creating pathways to ensure no child goes hungry.
