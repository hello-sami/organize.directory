Community Support

  1. NorCal Resist ↗ norcalresist.org

    A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives across Northern California. They organize direct assistance through food distribution, deportation defense, asylum support, and climate resilience programs while building networks of solidarity through their Seeds of Solidarity farm, brake light repair clinics, and community accompaniment services.

  2. Rural SURJ of Northern California ↗ facebook.com

    A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in rural Northern California. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations in rural communities.

  3. Northern California Innocence Project ↗ ncip.org

    A legal group that seeks to exonerate people wrongfully incarcerated by the state.

  4. Norcal Anti-War Action ↗ instagram.com

    A grassroots organization coordinating anti-war actions and peace initiatives across Northern California.
