St. Petersburg

Food Not Bombs St. Pete

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in St. Petersburg. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.

St. Pete Copwatch

Dedicated to holding law enforcement accountable in St. Petersburg and the greater Tampa Bay area.

St. Pete Tenants Union

A tenant-led organization building tenant power and housing justice through mutual aid and collective action in St. Petersburg. They coordinate tenant defense, housing advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through organizing, direct support, and creating spaces for housing rights and community solidarity.

Queer Expression

Queer Expression (QE) St Pete is a completely grassroots volunteer collective that provides multigenerational community spaces for Queer and Trans folks.
