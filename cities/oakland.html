Oakland

Prisoner Support

A.B.O. Comix

Organization supporting LGBTQ+ prisoners through art publications, direct financial assistance, and pen pal programs. Provides essential items, legal support, and resources while amplifying prisoner voices through comics and art.

Oakland Abolition & Solidarity

Grassroots collective organizing for prison abolition and building solidarity between communities inside and outside prison walls.

Critical Resistance

Building an international movement to abolish the prison industrial complex through grassroots organizing, coalition building, political education, and direct action. Their Oakland chapter leads campaigns against imprisonment and policing while developing community-based alternatives.

Environmental Justice

Asian Pacific Environmental Network (APEN)

Building power in Asian immigrant and refugee communities for environmental, social, and economic justice through grassroots organizing, mutual aid, and policy advocacy in Oakland and Richmond.

Housing Justice

Causa Justa :: Just Cause (CJJC)

Multi-racial, grassroots organization building community power through tenant rights advocacy, mutual aid, and organizing against gentrification and displacement in Oakland and San Francisco.

Youth Justice

Communities United for Restorative Youth Justice (CURYJ)

Youth-led organization building power through healing, leadership development, and community organizing to end youth incarceration and transform the criminal justice system through mutual aid and restorative practices.
