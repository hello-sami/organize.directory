Local Mutual Aid Initiatives

Tucson Food Share

A mutual aid organization providing food and resources to the Tucson community through food distributions and community support networks.

Revolutionary Grounds

A radical bookstore and community space fostering mutual aid, political education, and solidarity through literature, events, and organizing. They provide a hub for community building, resource sharing, and collective action in Tucson.

Tucson Mesh

An anti-capitalist community-controlled wireless internet network providing sliding-scale and low-cost internet access. They build community power through collective infrastructure, tech education, and mutual aid while ensuring equitable access to vital internet services without corporate surveillance or data collection.

Black Cat Community Center

A community space fostering mutual aid, solidarity, and collective organizing through events, workshops, and resource sharing. They provide a hub for community building, political education, and direct support while creating autonomous spaces for movement building and collective care.

Tucson Anti-Repression

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and community defense through solidarity networks and direct support in Tucson. They coordinate legal support, resource sharing, and community care initiatives while building power through collective action and creating spaces for resistance and community protection.
