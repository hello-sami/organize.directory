Local Mutual Aid Initiatives

Food Security

Boulder Food Rescue

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and food justice through just-in-time food rescue and redistribution in Boulder County. They coordinate food recovery by bike, no-cost grocery programs, and community-led food access initiatives while building power through participatory research, leadership development, and creating spaces for food system transformation.

Peace & Justice

Rocky Mountain Peace and Justice Center

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through nonviolent action and education in Boulder. They coordinate peace initiatives, environmental justice campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, political education, and creating spaces for movement building and social change.
