Sonoma County

Raizes Collective

Santa Rosa-based organization mobilizing and empowering the community through arts, culture, and environmental education. Supporting artists of color with resources and programming while facilitating healing within bilingual and bicultural communities.

UndocuFund

A collaborative mutual aid effort providing direct assistance and resources to undocumented immigrants in Sonoma County affected by disasters, hardships, and other challenges.

Peace & Justice Center of Sonoma County

A community center fostering mutual aid and solidarity through peace activism, social justice organizing, and community education. They coordinate events, publish the Peace Press, and provide resources while building networks of support through grassroots organizing, indigenous solidarity, and collective action for social change.
