Lavender Fund

Ward 2 Mutual Aid

A neighborhood-based mutual aid network providing direct support and resources to residents of DC's Ward 2. A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Washington DC. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

A community organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through organizing against police violence in Washington DC. Beloved Community Incubator

A community-based organization fostering mutual aid and economic justice through cooperative development in the DC metro area. They coordinate worker-owned business incubation, solidarity economy initiatives, and community wealth building while creating spaces for economic democracy, racial equity, and collective liberation through their various programs and support networks.

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Washington, DC.
