Washington DC

Lavender Fund

A mutual aid network providing direct support and resources to ensure reproductive healthcare access. They coordinate funding assistance, practical support, and community care while building power through reproductive justice organizing and solidarity networks.

Ward 2 Mutual Aid

A neighborhood-based mutual aid network providing direct support and resources to residents of DC's Ward 2. They coordinate food distribution, emergency assistance, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, direct support, and creating spaces for neighbor-to-neighbor aid.

SURJ DC

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Washington DC. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Harriet's Dreams

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through collective organizing in Washington DC. They coordinate direct support initiatives, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective liberation.

Stop Police Terror DC

A community organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through organizing against police violence in Washington DC. They coordinate direct action support, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective resistance.

Families and Friends of Incarcerated People (FFOIP)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through support for incarcerated people and their families in Washington DC. They coordinate direct support initiatives, advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for family support and collective care.

Beloved Community Incubator

A community-based organization fostering mutual aid and economic justice through cooperative development in the DC metro area. They coordinate worker-owned business incubation, solidarity economy initiatives, and community wealth building while creating spaces for economic democracy, racial equity, and collective liberation through their various programs and support networks.

Food Not Bombs DC

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Washington, DC. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.

Claudia Jones School

The Claudia Jones School for Political Education is a popular education organization aiming to build the political consciousness of the Washington, D.C. working-class community.

East of the River Mutual Aid

Part of DC Mutual Network. Representing ward 7 and 8

Free Minds Book Club

Free Minds uses the transformative tools of books, writing, and peacebuilding to create a community of support, assisting incarcerated and formerly incarcerated youths and adults to achieve their education, career, and life goals, write new chapters in their lives, and become powerful voices for change.

DC IWOC (Incarcerated Workers Organizing Committee

Resisting the state through an abolitionist inside-outside network, on occupied Piscataway, Manahoac, Appamattuck, Chesapeake & Tutelo lands
