Food Not Bombs Burlington

A mutual aid collective fostering food security and community solidarity through direct action in Burlington. They coordinate free food distribution, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through food justice, anti-militarism, and creating spaces for sustainable food access and community care.

Peace & Justice Center

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through education, advocacy, and activism in Burlington. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through human rights advocacy, economic justice, and environmental justice initiatives. Their work emphasizes creating spaces for movement building and meaningful peace within and beyond Vermont.
