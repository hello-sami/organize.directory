Maslow Project

Community organization providing comprehensive support for homeless youth and families through basic needs assistance, street outreach, family advocacy, case management, and permanent supportive housing programs.

Rogue Valley Mentoring

Community-based mentoring organization providing support, guidance, and resources to youth through mutual aid and solidarity networks.

Judi's Midnight Diner

Judi’s Midnight Diner is a volunteer-run project bringing a heated pop-up tent, food, and hot drinks to folks around the Rogue Valley, Oregon.
