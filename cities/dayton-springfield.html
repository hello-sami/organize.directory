Dayton-Springfield, Ohio

10 groups · Ohio

  1. Dayton Anti-Racist Network

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through anti-racist organizing in Dayton. They coordinate direct action support, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, racial justice education, and creating spaces for collective resistance against systemic racism.

  2. PFLAG Dayton

    We provide LGBTQ+ advocacy, education, and support in Dayton, Miami County, and the surrounding Miami Valley area for all Q+ humans and their allies.

  3. Closet TRANSformation

    A clothing exchange affirming the transgender community of the Miami Valley.

  4. Access to Excess

    Access to Excess is a food rescue organization in Dayton, Ohio. We work to reduce food waste in our community.

  5. Dayton Tenant Union

    We provide resources and support for tenants, including connecting them with attorneys, educating them about their rights, and advocating for improved housing policies.

  6. Dayton Community Bail Fund

    The Dayton Community Bail Fund helps people detained in Miami Valley County Jails absorb some of the financial burden of incarceration.

  7. Gem City Action

    Gem City Action is an organization devoted to advocating for human rights in Dayton, Ohio.

  8. Gem City DSA

    We are the Dayton-Miami Valley chaper of the Democratic Socialists of America.

  9. Visible and Resilient

    A Montgomery County Ohio LGBTQ+ youth program | affirmative queer space

  10. Montgomery County Jail Coalition

    We address injustice at the Montgomery County Jail in Ohio.
