Tallahassee Community Action Committee

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through collective organizing in Tallahassee. They coordinate direct action support, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for social justice and collective resistance.

Food Not Bombs Tallahassee

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Tallahassee. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.
