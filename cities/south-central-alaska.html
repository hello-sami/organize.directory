South Central Alaska

Regional Organizations

Food Bank of Alaska

A community organization fostering mutual aid and food security through food distribution networks in South Central Alaska. They coordinate food recovery, emergency assistance, and community support while building power through volunteer mobilization and creating pathways for food access across the region.

Anchorage Mutual Aid Network

A grassroots collective fostering community solidarity and support in the Anchorage area. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and community care initiatives while building power through collective action and creating spaces for mutual support and community resilience.
