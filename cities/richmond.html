Food Coalition Resource Center (FCRC)

A mutual aid collective coordinating food distribution and community support initiatives in Richmond. They organize direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building sustainable systems of community care through food justice and collective action.

Food Not Bombs Richmond

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Richmond. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.
