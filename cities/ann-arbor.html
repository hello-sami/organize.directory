Prison Support

Abolitionist Book Project

A mutual aid collective providing books and educational resources to incarcerated individuals in Michigan. They coordinate book donations, resource sharing, and direct support while building networks of solidarity through literature and education, working to break isolation and support learning and empowerment for people in prisons.

Community Spaces

Bridge Community Cafe

A community cafe fostering mutual aid and solidarity through accessible food and gathering spaces in Ann Arbor. They coordinate pay-what-you-can meals, community events, and resource sharing while building power through food justice, collective care, and creating spaces for connection and movement building.

Peace & Justice

Interfaith Council for Peace and Justice

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through interfaith organizing and peace activism in Ann Arbor. They coordinate direct action, educational programs, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice, environmental justice, and creating spaces for interfaith collaboration and social change.
