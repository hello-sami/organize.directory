Mutual Aid Networks

Uptown Wagon

A collective of neighbors who meet in uptown Manhattan to distribute meals & supplies to neighbors experiencing homelessness.

Food Not Bombs Lower East Side

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in New York City's Lower East Side. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.

Housing

East Harlem Preservation

A community organization promoting East Harlem's cultural, economic and housing needs through advocacy, community organizing, and education.

Met Council on Housing

A tenants' rights membership organization that fights for tenants' rights and affordable housing with a strong presence in Manhattan.

Food Support

NY Common Pantry

Reducing hunger and promoting dignity, health, and self-sufficiency with locations in East Harlem and the Bronx.

The Campaign Against Hunger NYC

Nonprofit operating food pantries and gardening workshops.
