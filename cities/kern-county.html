Kern County Justice Committee

A grassroots organization working to promote police accountability and community safety through advocacy, mutual aid, and organizing in Kern County. They support families impacted by police violence, coordinate community oversight efforts, and build power through education, policy reform, and collective action for justice.

Awareness into action. Solidarity not charity.
