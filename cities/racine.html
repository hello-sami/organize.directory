LGBT Center of SE Wisconsin

A community center providing mutual aid and support services for LGBTQ+ individuals in Southeast Wisconsin. They offer support groups, educational resources, advocacy services, and direct assistance while building connections between LGBTQ+ community members and allies. Their comprehensive resource network includes healthcare referrals, crisis services, and community programming.
