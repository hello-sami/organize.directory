Cincinnati

SURJ Cincinnati

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Cincinnati. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Grove House Community

A mutual aid collective in Cincinnati fostering community care and solidarity through shared resources and collective organizing. They coordinate direct support, community gatherings, and resource sharing while building sustainable networks of mutual aid and community power.

Trans Mentorship of Cincinnati

The Transgender Mentorship is working to bring support and friendship to transgender people in the Greater Cincinnati, OH area.

Transform Cincy

Trans Wellness

Heartland Trans Wellness Group is a Cincinnati, Ohio based project dedicated to creating accessible, affirming community resources for Midwestern trans, queer, and LGBTQPIA people.
