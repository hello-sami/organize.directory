Community Support

Amos House

A comprehensive social service organization providing mutual aid and direct support through their soup kitchen, food pantry, and housing programs. They combine immediate assistance with long-term support through job training, recovery services, and financial literacy programs to help build community resilience.

Community Organizing

Direct Action for Rights and Equality (DARE)

Member-led organization building power in low-income communities of color through mutual aid, direct action, and grassroots organizing for housing justice, police accountability, and workers' rights.
