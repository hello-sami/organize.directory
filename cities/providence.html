Providence, Rhode Island

7 groups · Rhode Island

  1. Amos House ↗ amoshouse.com

    A comprehensive social service organization providing mutual aid and direct support through their soup kitchen, food pantry, and housing programs. They combine immediate assistance with long-term support through job training, recovery services, and financial literacy programs to help build community resilience.

  2. Food Not Bombs Providence ↗ instagram.com

    A grassroots mutual aid collective dedicated to recovering and sharing free vegan meals in Providence. They coordinate food recovery, community meals, and resource distribution while building solidarity through direct action against hunger and food waste.

  3. Direct Action for Rights and Equality (DARE) ↗ daretowin.org

    Member-led organization building power in low-income communities of color through mutual aid, direct action, and grassroots organizing for housing justice, police accountability, and workers' rights.

  4. Red Ink Community Library ↗ redinkri.org

    A radical community library and mutual aid space providing free access to books, zines, and educational resources. Red Ink fosters community learning and solidarity through their lending library, workshops, and organizing space.

  5. Black and Pink Providence ↗ instagram.com

    RI chapter of @blackandpinkorg fighting alongside #LGBTQI2+ prisoners.

  6. Providence Organization of Workers and Renters ↗ powrpvd.org

    We are building the power of the working class in Providence by empowering people to take control of their neighborhoods, workplaces, and community institutions.

  7. Providence Youth Student Movement ↗ prysm.us

    PrYSM mobilizes queer Southeast Asian youth, families, and allies to build grassroots power and organize collectively for social justice.
