Greenville

WMXP 95.5 FM - Malcolm X Center Community Radio

The only FCC-registered low-power Black community-owned and operated radio station in South Carolina, providing independent local and national programming for and by Greenville's Black community since 2007. Operating through the Malcolm X Center For Human Rights, they focus on political education, strategic planning, and communications skill enhancement for human rights and self-determination advocacy.

PrideLink

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support for LGBTQ+ people in Upstate South Carolina. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, mental health support, and creating affirming spaces for queer and trans people to connect and thrive.

Upstate Food Not Bombs

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Greenville and the Upstate region. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.
