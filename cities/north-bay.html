Local Organizations

Russian Riverkeeper

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and environmental stewardship through watershed protection and community science in the Russian River region. They coordinate water quality monitoring, habitat restoration, and solidarity networks while building power through environmental education, climate resilience, and creating spaces for community-led river conservation.

North Bay Organizing Project

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through multi-racial, multi-issue organizing in the North Bay. They coordinate direct support, policy advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through collective action, leadership development, and creating spaces for social, economic, and environmental justice.
