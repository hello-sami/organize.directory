Racial Justice

SURJ Worcester

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Worcester and Central Massachusetts. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Food Security

Woo Fridge

A mutual aid collective coordinating community fridges and food distribution initiatives in Worcester. They organize direct support through free food access, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through food justice and community care.
