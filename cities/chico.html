Chico

NorCal Resist Chico

General antifascist community organizing. Runs a mutual aid distribution on Tuesdays.

Red Star Chico

Communist mass org for Chico and the surrounding area.

Chico Red Meals

The food distribution arm of Red Star Chico. Weekly distributions.

Stonewall Alliance of Chico

LGBT community center that has queer events, resources, and social groups.

Chico Palestinian Advocacy Network

Organizes local events to educate and build solidarity for a free Palestine.

Students for Justice in Palestine Chico

Student-led group based out of CSU Chico. Open to non-students.

South Chico Community Assistance Center

Community Center that runs a food pantry, a resilience hub for disaster preparedness, and a meeting space.

Safe Space Chico

Low-barrier emergency shelter that operates with a harm-reduction approach. Guests are not required to take drug tests and can keep personal belongings, pets, and stay with unhoused partners/family. They also operate warming/cooling shelters during extreme weather.
