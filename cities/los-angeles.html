Los Angeles, California

Worker Support

Garment Worker Center

A worker center organizing with garment workers in Los Angeles to build power and fight exploitation in the garment industry. Through mutual aid, direct action, and worker-led organizing, they provide support services, leadership development, and advocate for better working conditions and fair wages.

Women's Support

The Women's Protection and Resilience (WPV)

A mutual aid organization providing support, resources, and community care to women in Los Angeles through direct assistance and solidarity networks.

Community Support

LA Anarchists in Solidarity

A mutual aid collective organizing direct support and community care initiatives in Los Angeles. They coordinate resource sharing, solidarity networks, and direct action while building autonomous community power through collective organizing and mutual support.

International Solidarity

Eagle and the Condor Liberation Front (ECLF)

A grassroots collective organizing mutual aid and solidarity initiatives connecting Indigenous and non-Indigenous communities in Los Angeles. They coordinate direct support, cultural exchange, and community education while building networks of solidarity between North and South American Indigenous movements through collective action and ancestral wisdom sharing.

Cultural Centers

Tía Chucha's Centro Cultural

A cultural center and bookstore fostering mutual aid, arts education, and community empowerment in the San Fernando Valley. They coordinate literary events, indigenous ceremonies, youth programs, and community workshops while building power through cultural preservation, artistic expression, and creating spaces for transformative community learning.
