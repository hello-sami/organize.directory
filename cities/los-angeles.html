Eagle and the Condor Liberation Front (ECLF)

The Eagle and the Condor Liberation Front (ECLF) was envisioned at UCLA in October of 2017. Started primarily as a student organization, ECLF expanded beyond student politics to center the diverse Indigenous community of Los Angeles while working through the contours of the university. Since the organization's inception, the main focus of organizing has been conducted through political education, food sovereignty, internationalist solidarity, and public Indigenous speaker engagements.
