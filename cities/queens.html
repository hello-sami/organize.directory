Building power, housing stability, and economic well-being of South Asian and Indo-Caribbean communities in Queens.

Ridgewood Tenants Union

A grassroots tenant organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through tenant organizing and housing justice work in Ridgewood, Queens. They coordinate tenant support, housing advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through collective organizing, direct action, and creating spaces for tenant empowerment.
