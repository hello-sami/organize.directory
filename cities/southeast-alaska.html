AK SEAGLA

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support for LGBTQ+ individuals in Southeast Alaska. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, education initiatives, and creating safe spaces for LGBTQ+ community members.
