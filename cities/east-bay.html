Refugee & Immigrant Support

Center for Empowering Refugees and Immigrants (CERI)

Community organization providing culturally attuned mental health services, LGBTQ+ advocacy, youth programs, and community care for refugee and immigrant communities in Oakland, with a focus on Southeast Asian genocide survivors and their families.

Legal Support

Centro Legal de la Raza

Community legal organization providing direct legal services, community education, and advocacy for immigrant, tenant, and worker rights in Oakland, with a focus on serving low-income and immigrant communities through mutual aid and solidarity.
