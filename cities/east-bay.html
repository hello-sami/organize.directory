East Bay

Cities

Berkeley Oakland
East Bay Food Not Bombs

We are an all-volunteer collective that serves vegan food in Oakland and Berkeley six days a week. In addition to the seven hot meals we serve each week, we distribute bagged lunches and dinners out of our truck and bicycle across the East Bay.

Center for Empowering Refugees and Immigrants (CERI)

Community organization providing culturally attuned mental health services, LGBTQ+ advocacy, youth programs, and community care for refugee and immigrant communities in Oakland, with a focus on Southeast Asian genocide survivors and their families.

Centro Legal de la Raza

Community legal organization providing direct legal services, community education, and advocacy for immigrant, tenant, and worker rights in Oakland, with a focus on serving low-income and immigrant communities through mutual aid and solidarity.

Village of Love

A grassroots organization operating drop-in centers and providing direct support to unhoused neighbors in Alameda, Berkeley, and Oakland. They coordinate mutual aid initiatives, resource sharing, and community care while building networks of solidarity through direct action and neighborhood organizing.

JR Consulting

A community-based organization providing support and advocacy for families affected by police violence in the East Bay. Through mutual aid and direct support, they offer resources, counseling, and solidarity networks while building power through family advocacy, community organizing, and creating spaces for healing and justice.

Gill Tract Community Farm

A community-managed urban farm fostering food sovereignty and mutual aid through agroecology, education, and collective organizing. They coordinate food distributions, seed sharing, and community programs while building power through sustainable agriculture, environmental justice, and creating spaces for community-driven food systems.

East Bay Alternative Book and Zine Fest

A community-driven festival fostering mutual aid and solidarity through independent publishing and DIY arts. They coordinate an annual gathering of zinesters, artists, and radical publishers while building networks of creative resistance and supporting grassroots literary culture in the East Bay.

Albany for Palestine

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through Palestinian liberation organizing in the East Bay. They coordinate direct actions, community education, and solidarity networks while building power through local organizing, boycott campaigns, and creating spaces for Palestinian resistance and community mobilization.

East Bay Housing Organizations (EBHO)

A membership organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through affordable housing advocacy and community organizing in the East Bay. They coordinate resident leadership development, policy advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through grassroots organizing, faith-based partnerships, and creating spaces for housing justice.
