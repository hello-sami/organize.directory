Morgantown, WV

5 groups · West Virginia

  1. Appalachian Prison Book Project ↗ appalachianprisonbookproject.org

    APBP sends free books to people imprisoned in six Appalachian states (West Virginia, Virginia, Tennessee, Kentucky, Ohio, and Maryland), hosts book clubs inside prisons in our region, and is leading an effort to bring for-credit college courses into prison.

  2. Morgantown Mutual Aid ↗ instagram.com

    We are citizens of Morgantown working together to care for one another. We distribute supplies and connect community members with local resources.

  3. Morgantown Food Not Bombs ↗ instagram.com

    Morgantown WV's Food Not Bombs chapter. Free soup for the revolution.

  4. Morgantown Coalition for Housing Action (MoCHA) ↗ instagram.com

    Working to bridge gaps in resources & advocacy for our unsheltered neighbors in Morgantown, WV

  5. Project Rainbow WV ↗ projectrainbowwv.com

    Runs a a low-barrier 24-bed emergency shelter focused on housing while maintaining safety for unhoused members of the LGBTQ+ community.
