Food Security Initiatives

Food Not Lawns Corvallis

A grassroots initiative transforming lawns into sustainable food gardens, sharing resources, seeds, and knowledge while building food sovereignty through mutual aid networks in the Corvallis community.

Corvallis Folk School Community Food Program

Community-based organization offering food preservation workshops, community kitchen access, and skill-sharing programs to promote food security and self-sufficiency through mutual aid.

Student & Campus Initiatives

DAO Corvallis Mutual Aid

Student-led mutual aid network at Oregon State University coordinating resource distribution, community support, and solidarity actions for marginalized communities in Corvallis.

Human Services Resource Center

Campus-based organization providing food assistance, housing support, and basic needs resources to students through mutual aid principles and community partnerships.

Community Support

Community Outreach Inc.

Local organization providing emergency shelter, healthcare services, and support programs through mutual aid networks to assist community members experiencing homelessness or crisis.

United Way of Benton & Lincoln Counties

Coordinates mutual aid efforts and community resources to address housing insecurity, food access, and emergency assistance needs in the Corvallis area.

Corvallis Mutual Aid Network

Grassroots community network coordinating direct support, resource sharing, and solidarity actions to meet immediate needs of Corvallis residents through mutual aid principles.
