Biddeford-Saco

SURJ Biddeford-Saco Area

A chapter of the national SURJ network organizing white people for racial justice through mutual aid and community solidarity in the Biddeford-Saco area. They coordinate education initiatives, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through political education and accountable organizing.
