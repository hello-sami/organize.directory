Whites for Racial Equity

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Monterey County. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Old Capitol Books

An independent bookstore fostering mutual aid and community education through radical literature and cultural events. They coordinate free classes, reading groups, and community gatherings while building networks of solidarity through accessible education, anarchist theory, and creating spaces for collective learning and organizing.

Peace Resource Center

A community center fostering mutual aid and solidarity through peace education, social justice organizing, and nonviolent action in Monterey County. They coordinate events, workshops, and resource sharing while building networks of support through grassroots organizing, conflict resolution training, and creating spaces for collective action and community dialogue.
