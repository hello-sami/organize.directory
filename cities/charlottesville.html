The Beautiful Idea

A community mutual aid collective fostering solidarity and support through resource sharing, skill-building workshops, and direct community assistance. They work to create accessible spaces for organizing, education, and mutual support while building networks of care and resistance in Charlottesville.

Charlottesville DSA

Local chapter of the Democratic Socialists of America, the largest socialist organization in the country.

Charlottesville Gender Expansive Network

A community to provide a safe, enriching, and inclusive space for all trans, nonbinary, and gender expansive folks in the greater Charlottesville area

Cville Medic Collective

We check in on unhoused neighbors and medic for actions.

Charlottesville Food Not Bombs

We are an all-volunteer organization. We welcome new volunteers, to help with pickups of food donations, transport of food to our distribution site, or help at the distribution site.

Cville Litter Pickers

We do meetups where we litter pick in the Charlottesville and Albemarle county area for a few hours, then go socialize over lunch/dinner or drinks.
