The Reclamation Project

Youth-run grassroots organization building mutual aid initiatives, political education, and community healing programs for marginalized youth, with a focus on BIPOC, immigrant/refugee, and LGBTQ+ communities.

Re-entry support organization helping formerly incarcerated people transition back to the community through mutual aid and solidarity.

A self-governing homeless encampment in Sacramento that provides a safe, community-run space for unhoused individuals with support from local nonprofits and the city.

Homeless Solidarity

Sacramento Punks with Lunch

A mutual aid collective providing direct support and harm reduction services in Sacramento. They coordinate food distribution, resource sharing, and harm reduction supplies while building networks of solidarity through street outreach and community organizing.

Police Accountability

Anti Police-Terror Project (APTP) Sacramento

A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and community response initiatives in Sacramento. They organize direct support through Mental Health First (MH First) crisis response, community education, and solidarity networks while building alternatives to policing through collective action and transformative justice.

Peace & Justice

Sacramento Peace Action

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through peace education and nonviolent action in Sacramento. They coordinate direct action, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through anti-war activism, social justice organizing, and creating spaces for movement building.

Racial Justice

SURJ Sacramento

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Sacramento. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations like CHIRP (California Heritage: Indigenous Research Project) and NorCal Resist.
