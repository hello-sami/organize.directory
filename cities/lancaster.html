Mennonite Action Lancaster

A mutual aid collective organizing community support and solidarity initiatives in Lancaster. They coordinate resource sharing, direct support actions, and community education while building networks of care through faith-based organizing and social justice advocacy.

Lancaster County Chooses Love

Lancaster County Chooses Love is a grassroots organization that is committed to advocating for LGBTQ+ individuals within the community by creating safe social spaces, and connecting community members with local resources. Operating out of Lilitz.
