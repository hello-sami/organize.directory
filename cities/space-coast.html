Food Not Bombs Melbourne

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Melbourne, Florida. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.
