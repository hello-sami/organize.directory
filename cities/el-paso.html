La Mujer Obrera

A women-led organization that develops and celebrates Mexican heritage through economic development, community building, and mutual aid initiatives. They operate several social enterprises including Café Mayapán, Lummetik Trading Co., and Rayito de Sol Daycare, while maintaining programs focused on cultural preservation, food sovereignty, and community organizing through their Tierra es Vida Community Farm and various cultural programs.
