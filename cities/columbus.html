LGBTQ+ Support

Black Queer & Intersectional Collective (BQIC)

BQIC is a grassroots community organization in Central Ohio that works towards the liberation of Black LGBTQIA+ people from all walks of life through direct action, community organizing, education on our issues, and creating spaces to uplift our voices.

Community Support

Mutual Aid Street Solidarity (MASS)

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and community care through direct support initiatives in Columbus. They coordinate resource distribution, solidarity networks, and street outreach while building power through neighborhood organizing and creating spaces for collective support and resistance.
