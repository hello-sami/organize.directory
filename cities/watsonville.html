Pajaro Valley Prevention and Student Assistance

A community organization providing comprehensive mental health services, youth programs, and family support services. They work through mutual aid networks to ensure accessible mental health care and youth development resources for the Watsonville community.

Salud Para La Gente

A community health center operating on mutual aid principles to provide comprehensive healthcare services regardless of ability to pay. They offer medical, dental, and behavioral health services while advocating for health equity in the Pajaro Valley region.

Pajaro Rising

Mutual aid distribution and direct action for unhoused residents.

Community Bridges Lift Line

A community transportation program working through mutual aid networks to provide essential transportation services to seniors and people with disabilities, ensuring access to healthcare and community resources.
