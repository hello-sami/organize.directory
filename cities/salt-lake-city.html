Under the Umbrella

A queer-owned and operated bookstore providing a safe community space and mutual aid resources for the LGBTQ+ community in Salt Lake City. They offer a carefully curated selection of books centering queer voices and experiences, while fostering community connections and support through events, book clubs, and accessible pricing options.
