SURJ Marin

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Marin County. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
