Santa Cruz

Community Spaces

SubRosa

An anarchist community space and infoshop fostering mutual aid, radical literature, and collective organizing in Santa Cruz. They coordinate a lending library, host political education events, and provide space for community gatherings while building networks of solidarity through direct action and autonomous organizing.

Santa Cruz Hub for Sustainable Living

A constellation of mutual aid projects supporting skill-sharing and sustainable communities since 1994. The Hub hosts multiple initiatives including The Bike Church (community bike shop), The Fábrica (textile arts workspace), Hard Core Compost, PedX Courier & Cargo, and Tenant Sanctuary, while fostering interconnected networks of community care and ecological stewardship.

Housing Justice

Tenant Sanctuary

A tenant rights education project providing direct support and mutual aid through rights counseling, education, and community organizing. They offer in-person, phone, and email counseling on issues like rent increases, evictions, deposits, and habitability while building tenant power through collective knowledge and solidarity.

Immigration Support

Santa Cruz Welcoming Network

A community network dedicated to welcoming and supporting asylum seekers and refugees through mutual aid and direct support. They coordinate housing assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building community power through volunteer organizing and collective care for immigrants seeking safety and dignity.

Racial Justice

SURJ Santa Cruz County

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing across Santa Cruz County. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations in Santa Cruz, Watsonville, Aptos, Felton, Ben Lomond, and Boulder Creek.
