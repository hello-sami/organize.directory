Millions of Butterflies

A grassroots mutual aid collective fostering community support and solidarity in New Paltz. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and community care initiatives while building power through collective action and creating spaces for community resilience.

New Paltz Free Food Fridge

A community mutual aid initiative providing free food access at the Village Hall (25 Plattekill Ave). The fridge offers a space for neighbors to share food resources, operating on the principle that food is a human right and should be freely accessible to all community members.
