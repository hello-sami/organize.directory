SURJ Boston

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Boston. Operating as a program of Community Change Inc., they coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations since 1968.

OUT Metrowest

OUT MetroWest builds communities where LGBTQ+ youth thrive. Our free programs connect LGBTQ+ youth with supportive peers and LGBTQ+ adult role models.

Warm Up Boston

Homeless solidarity group that advocates for homeless survival through mutual aid distributions, court support programs, and demonstrations.

Boston Revolutionary Socialists

A revolutionary socialist organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through collective organizing in Boston. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective study and struggle.

Kataluma

A Boston refugee hospitality fellowship working to create safe and welcoming transitional lodgings for newly arrived refugees and asylum-seekers.

Boston Food Not Bombs

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid through free food distribution and community solidarity in Boston. They coordinate weekly food shares, resource recovery, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action against food waste and hunger, creating spaces for community gathering and collective care.

Allston-Brighton Community Fridges

A mutual aid network maintaining community fridges and pantries in the Allston-Brighton neighborhoods of Boston. They coordinate food distribution, resource sharing, and community care while building power through neighborhood solidarity and creating accessible spaces for food security.

Weelaunee Solidarity Boston

A solidarity collective organizing mutual aid and direct support for Indigenous land defense in Weelaunee (Atlanta Forest). They coordinate resource sharing, political education, and solidarity networks while building connections between Boston communities and Indigenous-led struggles for land sovereignty.

Boston Anarchist Book Fair

An annual gathering fostering mutual aid and radical education through literature, workshops, and community building. They coordinate book distribution, skill sharing, and solidarity networks while creating spaces for anarchist organizing, political education, and building connections between various mutual aid initiatives.
