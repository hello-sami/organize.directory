Des Moines Catholic Worker

A grassroots community fostering mutual aid and solidarity through hospitality, nonviolence, and direct service in Des Moines. They coordinate housing support, free meals, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community living, peace activism, and creating spaces for radical hospitality and social justice.

SURJ Des Moines

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Des Moines. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
