  2. Love Huntsville ↗ lovehsv.org

    We are workers organizing for better working conditions and wages. If you’re in Alabama or Southern Tennessee reach out and let’s talk about how we can collaborate on improving your workplace.

  4. The LGBTQ+ Closet ↗ huntsvillelaughs.com

    Rocket City Medic Collective (RCMC) is a group of activists and health care providers who offer medical support at protests and in our communities.

  6. Huntsville Bail Fund ↗ hsvbailfund.org

    Huntsville Bail Fund is dedicated to addressing the disproportionate harm of pretrial detention on marginalized communities in North Alabama. Huntsville Bail Fund helps our neighbors secure their freedom by providing direct aid and advocacy to arrested individuals and their families.

  7. SURJ Huntsville ↗ facebook.com

    A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Huntsville. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
