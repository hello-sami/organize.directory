Huntsville

Food Justice

Manna House

A community-driven organization fostering mutual aid and food justice through direct support in Huntsville. They coordinate food distribution, emergency assistance, and solidarity networks while building power through community care, resource sharing, and creating spaces for dignified access to food and essential supplies.

Housing Justice

Love Huntsville

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and housing justice through direct support and advocacy for Huntsville's unhoused community. They coordinate street outreach, hot meal distribution, health clinics, and solidarity networks while building power through policy advocacy, encampment support, and creating spaces for dignity and equity. Their programs include bi-weekly camp dinners with free health services, necessity drives, and partnerships with organizations like Huntsville Bail Fund and Rocket City Street Medics.

Labor Rights

Industrial Workers of the World - Huntsville

A member-led union fostering mutual aid and worker solidarity through grassroots organizing in Huntsville and Southern Tennessee. They coordinate workplace organizing, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through worker relationships, collective action, and creating spaces for labor education and organizing across industries. Their approach emphasizes building durable worker organizations through strong relationships and collective power.

LGBTQ+ Support

The LGBTQ+ Closet

A community initiative fostering mutual aid and LGBTQ+ support through free clothing and resources in Huntsville. They coordinate clothing distribution, gender-affirming supplies, and solidarity networks while building power through direct support, community care, and creating affirming spaces for LGBTQ+ individuals to access essential items.

Emergency Response

Rocket City Street Medics

A volunteer collective fostering mutual aid and community care through emergency medical support in Huntsville. They coordinate street medic services, first aid training, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action support, harm reduction, and creating spaces for community health and safety.

Criminal Justice

Huntsville Bail Fund

A community organization fostering mutual aid and criminal justice reform through direct support for pretrial detention in North Alabama. They coordinate bail assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through advocacy for jail population reduction and creating pathways for community safety beyond incarceration.

Racial Justice

SURJ Huntsville

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Huntsville. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
