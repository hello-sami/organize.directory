Berkeley

Slingshot Collective

A radical publishing collective producing independent media and mutual aid resources since 1988. Through their quarterly newspaper, radical organizer, and community space at Long Haul Infoshop, they foster alternative media creation, political education, and direct action organizing while building networks of solidarity and resistance in Berkeley and beyond.

Yalla Berkeley

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through Palestinian liberation organizing in Berkeley. They coordinate direct support actions, community resources, and solidarity networks while building connections between Berkeley communities and Palestinian liberation movements through direct action and community education.

Berkeley Mutual Aid

A volunteer-driven mutual aid network coordinating neighborly support and community care across Berkeley. They organize direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity initiatives including COVID-19 support, transportation help, food distribution, and emergency response while building sustainable networks of community care.

The Long Haul

A radical community center and infoshop fostering mutual aid and grassroots organizing since 1979. Located at 3124 Shattuck Ave, they coordinate free food distributions, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through their radical library, community events, and creating accessible spaces for political education and collective action. Their infoshop is open to the public and serves as a hub for radical projects, zine archives, and community gatherings.

La Peña Cultural Center

A vibrant cultural center fostering mutual aid and solidarity through arts, education, and social justice since 1975. They coordinate cultural events, music performances, and community programs while building power through Latin American and local cultural preservation, political education, and creating spaces for artistic expression and movement building.

Revolution Books

A radical bookstore fostering mutual aid and critical consciousness through literature, political theory, and community events. They coordinate book talks, study groups, and solidarity initiatives while building power through revolutionary education, cultural programming, and creating spaces for radical thought and collective learning.

Berkeley Free Clinic

A volunteer-run free clinic fostering mutual aid and solidarity through accessible healthcare since 1969. They coordinate medical services, dental care, and peer counseling while building power through community health education, harm reduction, and creating spaces for equitable healthcare access regardless of ability to pay.

Berkeley Copwatch

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community safety through police monitoring and accountability since 1990. They coordinate community patrols, know-your-rights trainings, and documentation of police conduct while building power through direct action, public education, and creating networks of community-based oversight and support for those impacted by police violence.

Awareness into action. Solidarity not charity.
