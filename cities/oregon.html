Racial Justice

Clackamas County Equity Coalition

A grassroots coalition fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through racial justice organizing in Clackamas County. They coordinate direct support, advocacy initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through community education, policy reform, and creating spaces for racial equity and systemic change.

Respond to Racism

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through anti-racist organizing in Lake Oswego. They coordinate monthly community meetings, educational initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through community education, youth empowerment, and creating spaces for racial equity and systemic change in Lake Oswego and Oregon.

Rural Organizing

Rural Organizing Project

A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through rural organizing across Oregon. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through their network of autonomous human dignity groups, creating spaces for rural communities to work for democracy and human dignity.
