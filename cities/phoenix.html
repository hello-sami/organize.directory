Phoenix

General Support

Mutual Aid Phoenix

A community network providing direct support and resources to Phoenix residents through mutual aid initiatives.

Healthcare Access

AZPACH

Arizona Palestine Community Health provides mutual aid and healthcare support to Palestinians and other marginalized communities in Arizona through direct assistance, resource sharing, and community solidarity.

Cultural Centers

Palabras Bookstore

A bilingual bookstore and community space fostering mutual aid, cultural exchange, and solidarity through literature, events, and organizing. They provide a hub for community building, resource sharing, and collective care in Phoenix.

LGBTQ+ Support

one-n-ten

A nonprofit organization providing mutual aid and support services for LGBTQ+ youth and young adults ages 11-24 across Arizona. They coordinate direct assistance, housing support, health resources, and community care while building safe spaces for queer youth to thrive through peer support networks, educational programs, and identity-affirming initiatives.

Indigenous Solidarity

O'odham Solidarity

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity with O'odham communities through direct support and Indigenous sovereignty initiatives. They coordinate resource sharing, land defense actions, and solidarity networks while building power through traditional knowledge, cultural preservation, and creating spaces for Indigenous resistance and community care.
