Montclair Mutual Aid

A community-led mutual aid network coordinating direct support and solidarity initiatives in Montclair. They organize resource sharing, food distribution, and community care while building networks of support through neighborhood organizing, direct assistance programs, and collective action to ensure everyone in Montclair has access to the resources they need.
