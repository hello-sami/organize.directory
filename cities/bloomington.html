Really Really Free Market

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support through free markets and resource sharing in Bloomington. They coordinate direct assistance, free item distribution, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for gift economy and collective care.

McLean County Foster Closet

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through support for foster families in McLean County. They coordinate resource distribution, direct assistance, and solidarity networks while building power through community care, family support, and creating pathways for foster children and families to thrive.

Lunch for Peace

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and community power through food justice initiatives in Bloomington-Normal. They coordinate free meal programs, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community nourishment, collective care, and creating spaces for food access and dignity.

Center for Sustainable Living

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through sustainability initiatives in Bloomington. They coordinate multiple projects including bike repair, food pantries, tool sharing, and solar energy while building power through skill-sharing, environmental justice, and creating spaces for sustainable community development.

Pages to Prisoners

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through book distribution to incarcerated people. They coordinate book donations, letter writing, and solidarity networks while building power through educational access, resource sharing, and creating pathways for learning and connection across prison walls.

Indiana Recovery Alliance

Shifts resources and power to people who use drugs to reduce the harms caused by racialized drug policy through direct action and advocacy.

n anarchist group focusing on solidarity with anarchist prisoners, prisoners of other liberatory struggles, and individuals engaged in prison rebellion.
