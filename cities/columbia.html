Really Really Free Columbia

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support through free markets and resource sharing in Columbia. They coordinate direct assistance, free item distribution, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for gift economy and collective care.

Columbia Medic Collective

A grassroots organization providing mutual aid and healthcare support in Columbia. They coordinate street medic services, health resources, and solidarity networks while building power through community care and creating pathways for health justice and collective wellbeing.
