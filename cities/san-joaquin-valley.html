San Joaquin Valley, California

Valley Improvement Projects (VIP)

A grassroots organization advocating for social and environmental justice in Stanislaus County and the Central Valley. Through mutual aid and community organizing, VIP works on clean air, pesticide reduction, clean drinking water, climate justice, zero waste initiatives, and tenant rights while focusing on supporting underrepresented and marginalized residents.
