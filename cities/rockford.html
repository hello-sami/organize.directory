Rockford Promise

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through educational access and support in Rockford. They coordinate scholarship programs, mentoring initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through educational equity, community investment, and creating pathways for local students to access higher education.

Bridges to Prosperity Northern IL

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through poverty reduction and economic empowerment in Northern Illinois. They coordinate workshops, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through upward mobility programs, cross-sector collaboration, and creating pathways for families to build stability and thrive.

815 Mutual Aid

815 mutual aid is an anti-capitalist collective dedicated to providing mutual aid in Rockford and the surrounding area through the Coronavirus crisis.
