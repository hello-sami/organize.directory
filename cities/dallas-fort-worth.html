SURJ DFW

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

DFW Anti-War Committee

A grassroots organization in DFW working to end our country's many wars.

Trans Pride Initiative

A community organization providing mutual aid and support for transgender and gender diverse persons in areas of healthcare, housing, employment, education, and public accommodations. They focus on prisoner support for Texas trans and queer prisoners, healthcare access, housing assistance, and community empowerment through various action committees and resource programs.

Community Movement Builders

A member-based collective fostering mutual aid and community power through Black working-class organizing in Dallas. They coordinate cooperative economics, tenant organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through political education, direct action, and creating spaces for community self-determination and collective liberation.

Southern Sector Rising

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and environmental justice through community organizing in Dallas. They coordinate environmental advocacy, coalition building, and solidarity networks while fighting environmental racism and economic inequities through education, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for community power building in South Dallas.

Stop Cop City Dallas

North Texas coalition to stop the realization of the militarized police compound planned to be built on the UNT Dallas campus, Oak Cliff, District 8.
