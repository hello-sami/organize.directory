Peace and Justice Action League of Spokane (PJALS)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through nonviolent organizing, racial justice education, and movement building in Spokane. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through their BOLD program, Young Activist Leaders Program, and creating spaces for cross-racial, intergenerational organizing centered on the leadership of impacted people.
