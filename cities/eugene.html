Eugene

SURJ Springfield-Eugene

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Springfield and Eugene. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

ROAR Center

A community-led mutual aid center providing resources, organizing space, and support for local initiatives. ROAR (Resource Organizing and Radical) Center serves as a hub for community organizing, skill-sharing, and direct support in Eugene.

Neighborhood Anarchists

We organize locally in Eugene / Springfield Oregon to help build a society where neighbors support each other to meet basic needs, individuals are free to follow their passions, and empowered communities collectively shape the future.

White Bird Clinic

White Bird Clinic operates CAHOOTS (Crisis Assistance Helping Out On The Streets), the Mobile Crisis and Medic response team for Eugene-Springfield’s Public Safety System.

CORE Eugene

CORE empowers, and advocates for young people in Lane County.
