Minneapolis / St. Paul, Minnesota

Local Mutual Aid Initiatives

Rainbow Collective for Change

An organization building mutual aid networks and community support for LGBTQ+ families with children in the Twin Cities. They coordinate direct support through playgroups, educational programs, and family resources while building inclusive spaces and advocating for LGBTQ+ family equity through community organizing and solidarity networks.

Women Against Military Madness (WAMM)

A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and peace initiatives in the Twin Cities. They organize direct support, resource sharing, and community education while building networks of solidarity through anti-war organizing, peace advocacy, and social justice campaigns.

Southside Harm Reduction Services

A mutual aid collective providing harm reduction services and community support in Minneapolis. They coordinate direct assistance through syringe access, naloxone distribution, and health resources while building networks of solidarity through peer support, community education, and advocacy for harm reduction approaches to public health.

Library Patrons Union

A grassroots collective organizing mutual aid and community support initiatives to protect and expand public library services in Minneapolis. They coordinate direct action, resource sharing, and community advocacy while building networks of solidarity through patron organizing, library worker support, and defending public spaces as essential community resources.

Futures Beyond Criminalization

A mutual aid collective organizing community-based alternatives to policing and incarceration in Minneapolis. They coordinate direct support through transformative justice practices, community safety initiatives, and resource sharing while building networks of solidarity through political education, community organizing, and advocacy for non-carceral solutions.

Showing Up for Racial Justice (SURJ) - Twin Cities

A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives focused on mobilizing people for racial justice in the Twin Cities. They organize direct action, resource sharing, and political education while building networks of solidarity through community organizing and racial justice advocacy.

Sex Workers Outreach Project (SWOP) Minneapolis

A mutual aid collective organizing direct support and advocacy for sex workers in Minneapolis. They coordinate resource sharing, community care, and solidarity initiatives while building networks of support through harm reduction, decriminalization advocacy, and community education.

Communities United Against Police Brutality (CUAPB)

A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives focused on addressing police brutality in the Twin Cities. They organize direct support for survivors, resource sharing, and community education while building networks of solidarity through advocacy, legal support, and policy reform campaigns. Their work includes crisis response, know-your-rights training, and maintaining a 24-hour hotline for reporting police misconduct.

St. Paul Mutual Aid

A grassroots network coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives in St. Paul. They organize direct assistance, resource sharing, and neighborhood solidarity through their Facebook group, connecting community members to help meet immediate needs while building sustainable networks of care and support.

Boneshaker Books

A volunteer-run radical bookstore in Minneapolis's Seward neighborhood coordinating mutual aid through accessible radical literature and community space. They organize resource sharing and political education while building networks of solidarity through their bookstore, events, and community programming. Their space serves as a hub for leftist politics, movement support, and collective organizing.
