SURJ Santa Barbara

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Santa Barbara. They coordinate anti-racism educational curriculum, workshops, and study-to-action groups while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Santa Barbara Tenants Union

We build tenant power by providing regular education on tenants’ rights through Tenants’ Rights and by hosting workshops and a weekly Tenant Help Desk.
