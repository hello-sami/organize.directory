Raleigh

Community Support

Raleigh Mutual Aid Hub (RUMAH)

A grassroots network coordinating mutual aid efforts in Raleigh through direct support, resource sharing, and community organizing. RUMAH connects neighbors to meet immediate needs while building sustainable systems of community care and solidarity.

Refund Raleigh

A grassroots coalition organizing for community-led investment and mutual aid in Raleigh neighborhoods. Through their A.C.O.R.N.S proposal and neighborhood organizing initiatives, they work to build power for participatory budgeting and direct community control over resources.

Food Insecurity

Food Not Bombs Raleigh

A volunteer collective dedicated to recovering and sharing free vegan meals with the community, while organizing against food waste and food insecurity. Through direct action and mutual aid, they work to build food sovereignty and community resilience in Raleigh.

Mutual Aid Free The Market Raleigh

A mutual aid collective organizing free markets to distribute food, clothing, hygiene items, and other essential resources to community members experiencing food insecurity and economic hardship. Through direct action and solidarity, they work to meet immediate needs while building community power.

LGBTQ+ Support

LGBT Center of Raleigh

A community center providing mutual aid, support services, and resources for LGBTQ+ individuals in Raleigh. Through programs like care coordination, youth and family support, trans & gender expansive services, and health & wellness initiatives, they work to build a more inclusive and supportive community while meeting immediate needs.
