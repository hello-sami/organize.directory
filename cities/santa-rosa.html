SubRosa

An anarchist community space and infoshop fostering mutual aid, radical literature, and collective organizing in Santa Rosa. They coordinate a lending library, host political education events, and provide space for community gatherings while building networks of solidarity through direct action and autonomous organizing.

Positive Images

An LGBTQIA+ community center fostering mutual aid and support through direct services, education, and community building in Sonoma County. They coordinate support groups, therapy resources, art programs, and leadership development while creating spaces for collective care, cultural events, and building power through queer youth organizing and solidarity networks.
