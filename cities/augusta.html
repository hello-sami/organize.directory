CSRA Peace Alliance

The Central Savannah River Area (CSRA) Peace Alliance was formed in July 2008 in Augusta, Georgia to organize the efforts of diverse groups of people in the Central Savannah River Area working for peace.

CSRA Coalition for Immigrant Rights

CSRA Coalition for Immigrant Rights promotes just immigration policies and welcoming cultural conversations which prioritize understanding & the dignity inherent in all human beings, regardless of immigration status.

People Protesting for A Cause

This is a safe space for women, LBGTQIA+, BIPOC, and minority groups to safely come together for organizing protest & connecting with like-minded people around Augusta, GA
