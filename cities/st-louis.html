St. Louis

Pro-Justice STL

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through advocacy and direct action in St. Louis. They coordinate community support initiatives, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through collective organizing and creating spaces for justice and systemic change.

Coalition Against Police Crimes and Repression (CAPCR)

A grassroots coalition coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives focused on addressing police accountability in St. Louis. They organize direct support, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, policy reform, and creating spaces for justice and healing.

The St Louis Queer Support & Healing

SQSH is a grassroots community-based collective of queer and trans St. Louisans. They run a queer support helpline.

THISTL (Trans Housing Initiative St. Louis)

THISTL is a trans-led organization aimed to tackle the systemic injustices that Trans humans face in the housing sector.

STL Mutual Aid

A network of organizers, healers, artists, community leaders, and everyday people coming together to deliver food and supplies, provide financial solidarity, offer emotional support, and connect people to their neighbors.

MO Network

Fighting for progressive change and equity with and for people who use drugs and those with substance use disorder.

The T STL

A holistic harm reduction program in St. Louis, MO
