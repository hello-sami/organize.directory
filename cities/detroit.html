East Michigan Environmental Action Council (EMEAC)

A Black-led environmental justice organization building community power through mutual aid, youth organizing, and environmental education. Based at the Cass Corridor Commons, they work to cultivate sustainable communities through food sovereignty, climate justice, and intergenerational leadership development.

Petty Propolis

Organization leveraging poetry, literacy workshops, and political education to pursue racial and environmental justice through community-centered initiatives.

Detroit Safety Team

A grassroots collective coordinating mutual aid and community safety initiatives in Detroit. They organize direct support through crisis response, de-escalation training, and community care while building networks of solidarity through transformative justice practices and alternatives to policing.

Metro Detroit SURJ

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Metro Detroit. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Detroit Eviction Defense

Provides housing legal support, organizes direct actions to prevent evictions, educates tenants about their rights, and connects people with resources to fight housing displacement

Trumbullplex

A radical housing collective and DIY venue fostering mutual aid and community power through art, music, and anarchist organizing in Detroit. They coordinate shows, workshops, skill-shares, and solidarity networks while building power through collective living, cultural resistance, and creating accessible spaces for community gathering and movement building.

Source Booksellers

An independent bookstore fostering mutual aid and community power through literature and cultural programming in Detroit. They coordinate book access, educational events, and solidarity networks while building power through non-fiction focused community education, Black literature, and creating spaces for learning and movement building.

Urban Praxis Workshop

A collective fostering mutual aid and community power through participatory research and direct support initiatives in Detroit. They coordinate community-based research, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through collective knowledge production and creating spaces for community-led solutions to urban issues.

Community Movement Builders

A member-based collective fostering mutual aid and community power through Black working-class organizing in Detroit. They coordinate cooperative economics, tenant organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through political education, direct action, and creating spaces for community self-determination and collective liberation.
