Orlando Community Fridge

A mutual aid network fostering food justice through community fridges and pantries across Orlando. They coordinate food distribution, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community care, food sovereignty, and creating spaces for collective nourishment and support.

Food Not Bombs Orlando

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Orlando. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.
