Cleveland

Black Lives Matter Cleveland

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through racial justice organizing in Cleveland. They coordinate direct action support, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective resistance and systemic change.

Jun Wang Foundation

A community organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through support for families affected by police violence in Cleveland. They coordinate direct support initiatives, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through family support, community organizing, and creating spaces for healing and justice.

LGBT Community Center of Greater Cleveland

As one of the nation's oldest LGBTQ+ community centers and among the most prominent organizations in the state, we offer high-quality programming, services, and events to meet your personal and professional needs.

Rhizome House

A community space fostering mutual aid through accessible education, arts programming, and cultural events. They provide a hub for community organizing, skill-sharing, and building solidarity while supporting local artists and grassroots initiatives through workshops, gatherings, and collaborative projects.

Burning River Anarchist Collective

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through anarchist organizing in Cleveland. They coordinate direct support initiatives, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective resistance and mutual support.

InterReligious Task Force on Central America

A solidarity organization fostering mutual aid and international solidarity through community organizing and direct support initiatives. They coordinate solidarity networks, political education, and resource sharing while building connections between Cleveland communities and social movements in Central America and Colombia.

Cleveland Pandemic Response

A group of neighborhood organizers and community members aiming to provide mutual aid and support to NEO residents during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Food Not Bombs Lake County and East Side CLE

We share free fresh fruits and vegetables twice a month in Willoughby, and twice a month on Cleveland’s east side.

Westside Cleveland Food Not Bombs

FNB is a national movement that opposes militarism and provides free food for the public, no questions asked.

The InterReligious Task Force on Central America (IRTF)

IRTF is a Cleveland-based interfaith group that promotes solidarity, human rights, and collective liberation.
