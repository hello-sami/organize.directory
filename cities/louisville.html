Louisville Showing Up for Racial Justice (LSURJ)

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Louisville. They coordinate community organizing, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education, direct action support, and creating spaces for undermining white supremacy as part of a multi-racial movement for justice.

Change Today, Change Tomorrow

A Black-led organization fostering mutual aid and community empowerment through direct support initiatives in Louisville. They coordinate food security programs, educational resources, and solidarity networks while building power through community-driven solutions, economic development, and creating spaces for sustainable change in Black and marginalized communities.

Collective Liberation Network

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through collective liberation work in Louisville. They coordinate direct support initiatives, community organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through intersectional organizing, resource sharing, and creating spaces for transformative justice and community care.

Louisville Workers Brigade

The Louisville Workers Brigade is dedicated to educating, supporting, and uniting working people in our community.
