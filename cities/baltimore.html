Racial Justice

SURJ Baltimore

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing across Baltimore City, Baltimore County, and Howard County. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Cultural Centers

Red Emma's

A worker-owned cooperative fostering mutual aid and community power through radical literature, food justice, and cultural programming in Baltimore. They coordinate a bookstore, cafe, event space, and solidarity networks while building power through accessible knowledge sharing, cooperative economics, and creating spaces for movement building and social change.

Court Support

Baltimore Courtwatch

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through court monitoring and advocacy in Baltimore. They coordinate court watching, data collection, and solidarity networks while building power through community oversight, transparency initiatives, and creating spaces for court accountability and justice system reform.

Food Justice

Food Not Bombs Baltimore

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Baltimore. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.
