Rural Organizing

Rural SURJ of Northern California

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in rural Northern California. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations in rural communities.

Indigenous Solidarity

Tongva Land

A mutual aid initiative fostering solidarity and support for Tongva people, the original inhabitants of the Los Angeles Basin and Southern Channel Islands. They coordinate land tax contributions, cultural preservation, and solidarity networks while building power through Indigenous sovereignty, land stewardship, and creating spaces for Tongva cultural revitalization.
