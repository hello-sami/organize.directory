Our Angel's Closet

A mutual aid initiative fostering community support through free clothing and essential items distribution in Norfolk. They coordinate resource sharing, direct support, and solidarity networks while building power through community care and creating spaces for collective well-being.

KUSP (Keep Us Safe Project)

A mutual aid collective fostering community safety and support through direct action and solidarity in Norfolk. They coordinate community initiatives, resource sharing, and support networks while building power through collective organizing and creating spaces for community empowerment.

757 Community Fridges

A mutual aid initiative providing free food access through community refrigerators in Norfolk and the Hampton Roads area. They work to address food insecurity through direct community support and solidarity, maintaining public fridges that anyone can access 24/7.

Eleanor's Norfolk

An intersectional feminist bookstore and bottle shop that serves as a community space and supports local mutual aid networks. They amplify traditionally silenced voices through literature and host community events while participating in the Hampton Roads Mutual Aid Network.

Southeastern Trans Center

Humanities Behind Bars

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and education through prison abolition work in Norfolk. They coordinate educational programs, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community support, political education, and creating spaces for learning and collective liberation.
