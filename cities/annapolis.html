Coming to the Table Annapolis

A community organization working to acknowledge and heal from the racial wounds of the past and present through dialogue, mutual understanding, and relationship building. They facilitate conversations and actions that promote racial healing and equity through truth-telling, reconciliation, and restorative justice practices.

PFLAG Annapolis

Support for the LGBTQ+ Community, Their Families, Friends, and Allies

The Annapolis Juneteenth Celebration

A community organization that coordinates the annual Juneteenth celebration in Annapolis, commemorating the emancipation of enslaved African Americans and celebrating Black history, culture, and achievement through educational events and community gatherings.

Annapolis Pride

Annapolis Pride's mission is to advocate for, empower, and celebrate the LGBTQ+ community in Anne Arundel County to live fully and authentically.
