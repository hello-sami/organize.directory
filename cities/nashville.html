Brooklyn Heights Community Garden

A community-led urban farm and mutual aid hub in North Nashville providing fresh food bundles to neighbors at no cost. Through intergenerational learning, food sovereignty initiatives, and community building, they work to address food apartheid while fostering connection and healing.

Nashville Community Fridge

A mutual aid network providing free, accessible food through community refrigerators across Nashville. Operating under the principle of "take what you need, leave what you can," they work to address food insecurity through direct community support.

Music City Migra Watch

A community defense network providing mutual aid and solidarity through immigration enforcement monitoring and rapid response. They coordinate community alerts, resource sharing, and direct support while building power through collective action and immigrant justice organizing.

Food Not Bombs Nashville

FNB Nashville is an independent volunteer-run organization dedicated to recovering and sharing free food.

Nashville Organized for Action and Hope (NOAH)

Uniting Nashvillians to fight for justice in our community

Elmahaba Center

Founded in June 2019, Elmahaba Center board, staff, and volunteers are the first Coptic-led organization that is inclusive, intersectional, and intergenerational, serving all Arabic-speaking immigrants, refugees, and their children and anyone who asks for help in Nashville.

Nashville Mutual Aid

Committed to building a practice of caring mutual aid in Nashville

Nashville People's Budget Coalition

A coalition of organizations gathered to organize for a Nashville that fosters safe and thriving communities through fully-funded and community controlled public goods and non-police crisis response.

Trans Aid Nashville

Providing aid, community, and building power for all transgender people in the greater Nashville Area.
