Seattle-Tacoma

Prison Abolition

Black and Pink SeaTac

A queer/trans prison abolition collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through prisoner support and advocacy. They coordinate letter writing, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through LGBTQ+ prisoner organizing, abolition education, and creating spaces for connection and support across prison walls.

Racial & Social Justice

Got Green

A South Seattle-based environmental justice organization led by people of color and low-income people. Through mutual aid and grassroots organizing, they work on food access, green jobs, and climate justice while building community power in the South End.

King County Equity Now

Community-led coalition working to achieve racial and economic justice through land acquisition, capacity building, and policy advocacy in historically Black neighborhoods.

The People's Assembly

A grassroots organization in Tacoma focused on community empowerment and social justice through mutual aid, education, and direct action. They work to build solidarity networks and create sustainable community support systems.

Undocumented & Immigrant Support

Superfamilia KC

Mutual aid group providing support and resources for undocumented communities.

Food Insecurity

Emerald Sea Food Not Bombs

Local chapter providing free vegan meals and mutual aid in Seattle.

International Solidarity

Kaibigan FFPS

Filipino Food Pantry Seattle building international solidarity through mutual aid and community support for the Filipino diaspora.

Sông2Sea

Vietnamese solidarity organization fostering political education and community building through study groups, cultural projects, and mutual aid.

Capybara Colectiva

A militant, grassroots org of Latin American diaspora fighting and exposing the u.s. imperialism that forces migrants to leave their homelands.

LGBTQ+ Support

Lifewerq Project

LGBTQ+ and BIPOC-led organization providing mutual aid, community support, and resources for queer and trans communities in Seattle.

Youth & Student Support

Seattle Student Union

Student-led organization uniting Seattle schools through collective action, winning resources for mental healthcare, COVID safety, and other community needs.

Prison Support

Books to Prisoners

A Seattle-based nonprofit fostering mutual aid through free book distribution to people in prison nationwide. They coordinate book donations, letter writing, and solidarity networks while building connections through literature and education across prison walls.
