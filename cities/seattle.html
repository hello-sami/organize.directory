People's Harm Reduction Alliance (PHRA)

A community-based mutual aid organization providing vital harm reduction services and support to people who use drugs since 2007. Through their various programs including syringe exchange, naloxone distribution, and mobile outreach, they work to empower drug users and provide compassionate, non-judgmental care while building networks of community support across Seattle and King County.

Pipsqueak Seattle

A mutual aid collective providing free groceries, prepared meals, and essential supplies to community members in need. Through their food distribution programs and community pantry initiatives, they work to address food insecurity while building networks of solidarity and direct support in Seattle.

Wa Na Wari

A Black cultural center and mutual aid hub housed in a 5th-generation Black-owned home, creating space for Black ownership, possibility, and belonging through art, historic preservation, and community connection. They provide community meals, host art exhibitions, maintain community gardens, and offer various programs while working to preserve Black presence and combat displacement in Seattle's Central District.

Estelita's Library

A social justice community library and bookstore providing access to radical literature, educational resources, and mutual aid through their community space. They focus on Black and Brown liberation texts while fostering community learning, political education, and solidarity through their lending library, events, and pay-what-you-can book program.

Coalition of Anti-Racist Whites (CARW)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Seattle. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education, accountable organizing, and creating spaces for white people to work for racial justice in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

South Asians Resist

Militant org of South Asians organizing against caste, class, imperialism.

Seattle Revolutionary Socialists

A revolutionary socialist organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through collective organizing in Seattle. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective study and struggle.

Feminists for Jina PNW-Seattle

Intersectional and transnational feminists from Iran in diaspora and exile, in solidarity with the revolutionary people around the world.

1312 Press

Small anarchist zine-distro and publisher
