People's Organization for Progress (POP)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through advocacy and direct action in Newark. They coordinate community support initiatives, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through collective organizing, civil rights advocacy, and creating spaces for social and economic justice.

Ironbound Community Corporation

A community development organization engaged in collective action, mutual aid, and advocacy in Newark's Ironbound neighborhood. ICC works on environmental justice, affordable housing, immigration support, youth programs, and community organizing while building resident power for social change.
