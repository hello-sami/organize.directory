Vallejo Housing Justice Coalition

We believe that housing is a human right, We envision a city with housing that is affordable and reflects the needs of our community!

Vallejo Unity Garden

We are a community garden and educational space working to be a model and resource for sustainable local food production in the hands of the people.

Vallejo Free Store

just a humble little free store that's supported by our community. we're part of the @foodisfreesolano network of distribution sites.

The Sean Monterrosa Project

The Sean Monterrosa Project (TSMP) is a movement for justice, accountability, and community empowerment, founded in response to the police killing of Sean Monterrosa on June 2, 2020, in Vallejo, California.
