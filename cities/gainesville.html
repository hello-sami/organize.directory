Cultural Centers

Civic Media Center

A grassroots library and community space fostering mutual aid and radical education through alternative media and cultural programming in Gainesville. They coordinate book lending, film screenings, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through accessible information sharing, community organizing, and creating spaces for movement building and social change.

Food Justice

Food Not Bombs Gainesville

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Gainesville. They coordinate regular meal distributions, food recovery initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action, community care, and creating spaces for food sovereignty and collective nourishment.
