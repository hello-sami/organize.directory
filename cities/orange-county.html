OC Rapid Response Network

A community-led network providing rapid response support and resources for immigrants facing detention or deportation in Orange County. They operate a 24/7 hotline (714-881-1558), coordinate family preparedness planning, offer legal consultations, and build community power through mutual aid and solidarity networks.
