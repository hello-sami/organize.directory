Birmingham

Young Pioneers Birmingham

A youth-led organization fostering mutual aid and community power through political education and direct support in Birmingham. They coordinate solidarity initiatives, resource distribution, and youth organizing while building power through collective action, community education, and creating spaces for youth leadership and mutual support.

SURJ Birmingham

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Birmingham. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Alabama Books to Prisons Project

Based in Birmingham, this mutual aid initiative provides books and educational materials to incarcerated individuals, facilitates penpal connections, and publishes an Inside Out Newsletter. Run by Burdock Book Collective, they work to reduce isolation in prison life and increase access to educational resources.
