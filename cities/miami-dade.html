Miami Workers Center

A grassroots organization building power with domestic workers, tenants, and low-income communities through mutual aid, leadership development, and organizing for economic and gender justice. They provide direct support while fighting for systemic change through community-led campaigns.

Catalyst Miami

A community organization fostering mutual aid and economic justice through comprehensive support programs across Miami-Dade County. They coordinate prosperity services, climate resilience initiatives, and community economic development while building power through leadership training, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for community-driven change focused on health, housing, and environmental justice.

Beyond the Bars

Worker-led organization building power with criminalized workers through mutual aid, advocacy, and organizing to transform the labor movement and end employment discrimination.

Power U Center for Social Change

A youth-led organization fostering mutual aid and community power through organizing for racial, gender, and educational justice in Miami. They coordinate leadership development, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through youth organizing, transformative justice, and creating spaces for young people to lead movements for social change.

Miami Food Not Bombs

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid through free food distribution and community solidarity in Miami. They coordinate weekly food shares, resource recovery, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action against food waste and hunger, creating spaces for community gathering and collective care.
