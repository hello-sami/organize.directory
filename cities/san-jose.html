Peace & Justice

San Jose Peace & Justice Center

A resource center and community space supporting peace and justice movements in the South Bay area through mutual aid and solidarity networks. They provide freely available community space, printing resources, audio equipment, and organizing support while building power through grassroots activism and collective action.

Food Justice

San Jose Food Not Bombs

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through free food distribution and anti-hunger organizing. They coordinate food recovery, community meals, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action against food waste and food insecurity in the South Bay area.

Youth Support

Youth Space

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support for LGBTQ+ youth in San Jose. They coordinate direct assistance, mental health resources, and solidarity networks while building power through peer support, youth leadership development, and creating safe spaces for queer and trans young people to thrive.
