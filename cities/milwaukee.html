Milwaukee Mutual Aid

A grassroots network coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives in Milwaukee. They organize direct assistance, resource sharing, and neighborhood solidarity while building sustainable networks of care and support through community organizing and collective action.

Milwaukee Community Fridge

A mutual aid collective organizing free food access and community support through public refrigerators in Milwaukee. They coordinate food distribution, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through food justice, community care, and creating spaces for sustainable food access.

Milwaukee Mutual Aid

A grassroots network fostering community solidarity and direct support across Milwaukee through mutual aid organizing. They coordinate resource sharing, emergency assistance, and community care while building networks of support through local organizing and collective action.

Milwaukee Community Care and Mutual Aid

Milwaukee Community Care and Mutual Aid was organized to provide collaborative care and information to community members who may be most affected during these uncertain times.

Black and Pink Milwaukee

We are a collective in Milwaukee, WI organizing from an abolitionist feminist praxis to support currently and formerly incarcerated people who identify across the LGBTQ+ spectrum and/or living with HIV/AIDS.

Cooperation Milwaukee
