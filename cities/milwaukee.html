Milwaukee, Wisconsin

Local Mutual Aid Initiatives

Cooperation Milwaukee

A cooperative organization working to build autonomous mutual aid networks and community self-management in Milwaukee. They coordinate various mutual aid projects focused on creating sustainable, community-driven solutions for local needs.

The Community

A grassroots mutual aid organization in Milwaukee focused on building community power through direct support, resource sharing, and collective action. They organize mutual aid initiatives and community solidarity projects to address local needs and support Milwaukee residents.

Milwaukee Liberation Center

A community book center and organizing hub focused on political education, community development, and building alternative visions for a cooperative society. They provide mutual aid through educational resources, cultural programming, and community organizing while serving as a forum for collective action and solidarity building.

Milwaukee Freedom Fund

A Black-led organization providing mutual aid through bail support, legal education, and advocacy for protesters and community members impacted by pretrial detention. They work to end mass incarceration while providing direct support to reunite families and challenge systems that criminalize race and poverty.

Healthcare Workers for Palestine Milwaukee

A collective of healthcare workers organizing mutual aid and solidarity support for Palestine. They coordinate medical resource sharing, health education initiatives, and direct support while advocating for healthcare justice and building connections between medical workers and Palestinian communities.

Milwaukee 4 Palestine

A community organization coordinating mutual aid and solidarity efforts in support of Palestine. They organize local initiatives and build connections between Milwaukee communities and Palestinian solidarity movements.

Milwaukee Anti-War Committee

A grassroots organization working to build anti-war consciousness and solidarity through community organizing, mutual aid, and direct action. They coordinate local initiatives to support peace and justice movements while connecting Milwaukee communities with international solidarity efforts.

Milwaukee Mamas 4 Liberation

A collective of mothers and caregivers organizing mutual aid and community support initiatives in Milwaukee. They coordinate resource sharing, direct support, and solidarity actions while building networks of care and support for families in the community.

Voces de la Frontera

A Milwaukee-based grassroots organization led by low-wage and immigrant workers, organizing for worker and immigrant rights through mutual aid, direct support, and community power building. They provide essential resources including legal services, citizenship classes, worker rights education, and coordinate solidarity networks while advocating for systemic change.

Sun Seeker MKE

A Milwaukee-based mutual aid collective focused on community care and environmental justice. They organize resource sharing, sustainability initiatives, and direct support while building networks of solidarity and ecological awareness in the community.

Reproductive Justice Action Milwaukee

A grassroots collective organizing mutual aid and community support for reproductive healthcare access in Milwaukee. They coordinate resource sharing, direct support, and solidarity networks while advocating for reproductive justice and healthcare accessibility.

Milwaukee Alliance

A grassroots organization building community power through mutual aid and collective action in Milwaukee. They coordinate resource sharing, direct support initiatives, and solidarity networks while organizing for social justice and community empowerment.

Milwaukee Socialist Alternative

A local chapter organizing mutual aid and community support initiatives while building working class power through direct action and solidarity networks. They coordinate resource sharing, political education, and community organizing while connecting various social movements and providing material support to communities in need.

Milwaukee for Yemen

A grassroots collective organizing mutual aid and solidarity initiatives in support of Yemen. They coordinate community resources, direct support actions, and solidarity networks while building connections between Milwaukee communities and Yemeni humanitarian efforts.
