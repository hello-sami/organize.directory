Ithaca

3 groups · New York

  1. Alternatives Library ↗ alternativeslibrary.org

    A community-supported library fostering mutual aid through accessible education and radical literature. They provide a diverse collection of independent media, host educational programs, and create spaces for community learning while building networks of knowledge sharing and social justice resources.

  2. Tompkins County Workers' Center ↗ tcworkerscenter.org

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and worker power through education, organizing, and direct support in Ithaca. They coordinate worker rights advocacy, living wage campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through workplace organizing, community education, and creating spaces for dignified and just workplaces.

  3. No Mas Lagrimas ↗ nmlagrimas.wordpress.com

    A non-profit organization providing mutual aid and direct support to individuals and families in Tompkins County. They coordinate food rescue and distribution, community celebrations, youth programs, and support for domestic violence victims and recently incarcerated individuals. Regular food distribution takes place Thursdays 4-6pm at the Henry Saint John Building, with additional food box deliveries throughout Tompkins County.
