Bellingham, WA

5 groups · District of Columbia

  1. Community to Community Development (C2C) ↗ foodjustice.org

    Women-led grassroots organization building food sovereignty and farmworker power through mutual aid, agroecology, and solidarity organizing in Bellingham and Skagit Valley.

  2. Bellingham Unity Committee ↗ bellinghamunitycommittee.org

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through direct support and organizing in Bellingham. They coordinate resource sharing, community education, and solidarity networks while building power through collective action and creating spaces for community care and support.

  3. Bellingham Occupied Protest ↗ instagram.com

    A mutual aid collective that emerged from protest organizing to provide community support through resource distribution, food security initiatives, and direct assistance to marginalized communities in Bellingham.

  4. Sunnyland Free Pantry ↗ instagram.com

    Mutual aid free pantry in Sunnyland neighborhood providing food, hygiene items, and other essentials to community members in need through a 24/7 accessible community pantry.

  5. Unchain Whatcom ↗ unchainwhatcom.com

    We are a group of abolitionists working to abolish the Prison Industrial Complex (PIC) in Whatcom County through our pen-pal program, working to stop the construction of a new mega jail in Whatcom County, and fighting to improve the material conditions of people inside the Whatcom County Jail and Interim Work Center.
