Add a group.

This directory grows because people like you send in groups.

Know a mutual aid network, tenant union, bail fund, harm reduction crew, anti-war group, or any other leftist or grassroots organization we're missing? Send it in and we'll add it.

What we need

  • The group's name and a link (website, Instagram, Signal group, whatever they use).
  • The city or region they organize in — or "national" / "international" if they aren't locality-bound.
  • What they focus on (housing, food, labor, defense, etc.) — one or two sentences is plenty.

What we don't list

We keep this directory to groups doing material, on-the-ground organizing or solidarity work. We don't list: electoral campaigns, nonprofits that exist mostly to raise money, law enforcement collaborations, or groups that do harm to the communities they claim to serve. If you're not sure, send it anyway — we'd rather talk it through.
