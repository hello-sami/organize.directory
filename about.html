About This Directory

This directory is a resource for finding and connecting with mutual aid networks and grassroots initiatives across different cities and focus areas.

What is Mutual Aid?

Mutual aid is a form of community support where people work together to meet each other's needs, based on the principles of solidarity and reciprocity rather than charity.

How to Use This Directory

  • Search by city to find initiatives in your area
  • Browse by issues to find organizations working on specific causes
  • Connect directly with organizations through their provided contact information

Contributing

This is an open directory. If you're part of a mutual aid network or know of one that should be listed here, please contact us to have it added.
