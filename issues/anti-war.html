Anti-War & Peace Initiatives

Mutual aid networks and initiatives focused on anti-war organizing, peace building, and international solidarity.

Youth Organizing

Dissenters

A youth-led movement building local teams of young people across the country to divest from war and militarism. Through mutual aid and direct action, they organize to reclaim resources from the war industry to invest in life-giving institutions that truly keep people safe.

International Networks

Resist US-Led War Movement

Network of anti-war and pro-peace organizations building solidarity to oppose militarism, support just peace, and redirect resources from war to community needs.

Veterans for Peace

About Face: Veterans Against War

Organization of post-9/11 service members and veterans using direct action and mutual aid to end militarism, resist endless war, and build solidarity with communities impacted by U.S. militarism at home and abroad.

Direct Action

National War Tax Resistance Coordinating Committee (NWTRCC)

A coalition fostering mutual aid and resistance through war tax resistance and peace activism. They coordinate direct action campaigns, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through conscientious objection, anti-militarist organizing, and creating spaces for nonviolent resistance to war and military spending.
