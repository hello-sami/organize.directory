LGBTQ+ Support

Legal Support

Lambda Legal

National organization fighting for civil rights of LGBTQ+ people and everyone living with HIV through litigation, education, and public policy work.

Prisoner Support & Art

A.B.O. Comix

Organization supporting LGBTQ+ prisoners through art publications, direct financial assistance, and pen pal programs. Provides essential items, legal support, and resources while amplifying prisoner voices through comics and art.

Prison Justice

TGI Justice Project (TGIJP)

Black trans-led organization building a united family of transgender, gender-variant and intersex people inside and outside of prisons through mutual aid, direct services, leadership development, and cultural resistance. Their work includes re-entry support, legal advocacy, housing assistance, and political education to fight against imprisonment and create a world rooted in self-determination and gender justice.

Trans Support

Trans Women of Color Collective (TWOCC)

Organization providing mutual aid, community support, and advocacy for trans women of color.

Transgender Law Center

Organization providing legal aid, advocacy, and mutual support for transgender and gender nonconforming communities.

Trans Lifeline

Trans-led organization providing direct emotional and financial support to trans people in crisis.

Black Trans Travel Fund

A mutual aid organization providing resources and financial support to help Black trans women access safe transportation options. They coordinate direct assistance through travel funds, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building community safety and support systems for Black trans women navigating public spaces.

Regional Support & Organizing

Southerners on New Ground (SONG)

A regional Queer Liberation organization made up of LGBTQ people of color, immigrants, undocumented people, people with disabilities, working class and rural and small town people in the South. They coordinate mutual aid, direct support, and community organizing while building intersectional movements for social justice through leadership development, coalition work, and transformative organizing.

Funding & Resources

Queer Mobilization Fund

A grassroots community re-granting fund providing mutual aid and resources to queer and trans people of color organizing for liberation in the South. They coordinate direct support through grants, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power for and within queer communities through frontline organizing and movement building.
