Indigenous Solidarity

Environmental Justice

Indigenous Environmental Network (IEN)

Alliance of Indigenous Peoples protecting Earth Mother from contamination and exploitation through grassroots organizing, direct action, and building just transition initiatives. Leading campaigns against fossil fuel projects, carbon trading, and false climate solutions while advancing Indigenous knowledge and sovereignty.

National Organizations

Indigenous Anarchist Federation (IAF)

A grassroots federation coordinating mutual aid and solidarity initiatives in support of Indigenous sovereignty and liberation. They organize direct action, resource sharing, and political education while building networks of solidarity through anti-colonial resistance and Indigenous anarchist perspectives.
