Migrant Solidarity Networks

Mutual aid networks and initiatives focused on supporting migrants through solidarity, direct support, and community organizing.

Black Migrant Support

ABISA

Organization building solidarity networks and mutual aid initiatives to support Black migrants, while working to decolonize oppressive systems and foster communities that uphold dignity, safety, and justice.

Legal Support

Al Otro Lado

A binational advocacy organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through direct legal and humanitarian support at the US-Mexico border. They coordinate legal services, humanitarian assistance, and solidarity networks while building power through community advocacy, migrant rights defense, and creating pathways for justice and dignity in the borderlands.

Justice Action Center

A legal advocacy organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through impact litigation and community organizing for immigrant justice. They coordinate legal support, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through strategic litigation and creating systemic change.

Immigrant Defense Project

providing legal resources, fighting unjust laws, supporting detained individuals, and advocating for fair immigration policies.

National Networks

National Network for Immigrant and Refugee Rights (NNIRR)

A national network fostering mutual aid and solidarity through immigrant and refugee rights advocacy since 1986. They coordinate community education, policy advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through grassroots organizing, human rights documentation, and creating spaces for transformative movement building across diverse immigrant communities.

National Day Laborer Organizing Network

NDLON improves the lives of day laborers, migrants and low-wage workers. We build leadership and power among those facing injustice so they can challenge inequality and expand labor, civil and political rights for all.
