Abortion Access

National Organizations

National Network of Abortion Funds

Network of organizations that help people access and pay for abortions, providing financial assistance and practical support.

Plan C

Information about how to access at-home abortion pill options online.

I Need an A

Directory of verified abortion clinics, information about financial assistance, and logistical support.

Repro Care

Connects people seeking abortions with community support for practical needs like transportation, lodging, childcare, and emotional support.

Repro Legal Defense Fund

Provides legal support and defense funds for people facing criminalization for self-managed abortion.

Regional Organizations

Yellowhammer Fund

A reproductive justice organization fostering mutual aid and healthcare access through abortion funding and support in Alabama and Mississippi. They coordinate financial assistance, practical support, and solidarity networks while building power through reproductive justice education, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for bodily autonomy and community care. Their work includes funding abortion care, providing emergency contraception, and supporting people across the Deep South in accessing reproductive healthcare.

SC Abortion Access Collective

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support for reproductive justice in South Carolina. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for reproductive healthcare access and support.

Midwest Access Coalition

MAC supports abortion seekers traveling to/from/within the Midwest by arranging + funding travel, hotel, meals and babysitting
