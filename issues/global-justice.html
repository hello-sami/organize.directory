Global Justice & International Solidarity

Mutual aid networks and initiatives focused on global justice, anti-imperialism, and international solidarity.

International Networks

International League of Peoples' Struggle (ILPS)

Global anti-imperialist alliance building international solidarity and supporting democratic struggles worldwide through mutual aid and collective action.

Syrian American Medical Society (SAMS)

A global medical relief organization providing crucial healthcare services and organizing mutual aid networks in conflict zones and underserved regions. They coordinate medical missions, emergency response, and direct support while building sustainable healthcare infrastructure and training local medical professionals.

Asranarshism

A platform dedicated to anarchist perspectives and analysis on global justice issues, with a focus on international solidarity and radical political theory.

Grassroots Global Justice Alliance

A national alliance fostering mutual aid and solidarity through grassroots movement building and international solidarity. They coordinate climate justice, feminist organizing, and global well-being initiatives while building power through transnational movement connections and creating spaces for transformative community learning.
