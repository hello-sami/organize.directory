Agrarian Trust

A national land trust fostering mutual aid and land justice through community land ownership and stewardship opportunities. They coordinate land access initiatives, support next-generation farmers, and build solidarity networks while working towards equitable access, cultural reconnection, and regenerative agriculture through their Agrarian Commons model and community-based land ownership programs.
