    Network of collectives serving free vegan and vegetarian food to others. Each chapter is independent and autonomous.

    Works to reduce food waste through food recovery and redistribution while creating jobs in the process.

    Coalition of worker-based organizations whose members plant, harvest, process, pack, transport, prepare, serve, and sell food.
