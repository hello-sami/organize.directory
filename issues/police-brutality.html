Police Brutality Resources & Support

Legal Support & Documentation

Michael Nida II

A resource dedicated to documenting cases of police brutality and supporting families affected by police violence. Through mutual aid and advocacy, they work to expose police misconduct, provide support for victims' families, and build networks of solidarity for those impacted by state violence.

Community Support & Action

Families United 4 Justice Group

A grassroots network of families and communities affected by police violence, coordinating mutual aid and support initiatives. They organize direct support, advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through collective healing, family support, and creating spaces for justice and accountability.
