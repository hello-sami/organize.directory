Palestinian Solidarity

National Initiatives

Palestinian Youth Movement

Transnational, independent, grassroots movement of young Palestinians organizing for the liberation of Palestine and the return of all Palestinian refugees.

International Solidarity Organizations

International Jewish Anti-Zionist Network (IJAN)

A network of Jews fostering mutual aid and solidarity through anti-colonial and anti-imperialist organizing. They coordinate direct actions, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through Jewish participation in Palestinian liberation struggles and creating spaces for collective resistance against Zionism and oppression.

Rebuilding Alliance

A humanitarian organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through direct support to Palestinian communities. They coordinate rebuilding projects, emergency relief, and advocacy initiatives while building power through grassroots partnerships, policy change campaigns, and creating spaces for Palestinian voices to be heard by Congress and decision-makers.

Right of Return

Al-Awda: The Palestine Right to Return Coalition

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through Palestinian right of return advocacy and community organizing. They coordinate direct support initiatives, educational programs, and solidarity networks while building power through Palestinian diaspora organizing, anti-colonial resistance, and creating spaces for Palestinian cultural preservation and collective liberation.
