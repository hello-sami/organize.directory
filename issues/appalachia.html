Appalachian Community Fund

A community foundation fostering mutual aid and social justice through grassroots grantmaking in Central Appalachia. They coordinate funding support for organizations working on anti-racism, environmental justice, health equity, and youth leadership while building power through community-led change, social enterprise development, and creating spaces for lasting social transformation. Their approach emphasizes "Change, Not Charity," supporting initiatives that address the root causes of poverty and injustice.
