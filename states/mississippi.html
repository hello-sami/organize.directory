Statewide Organizations

Yellowhammer Fund

A reproductive justice organization fostering mutual aid and healthcare access through abortion funding and support in Alabama and Mississippi. They coordinate financial assistance, practical support, and solidarity networks while building power through reproductive justice education, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for bodily autonomy and community care. Their work includes funding abortion care, providing emergency contraception, and supporting people across the Deep South in accessing reproductive healthcare.

Mississippi Immigrants Rights Alliance (MIRA)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through immigrant rights advocacy and support across Mississippi. They coordinate direct assistance, legal resources, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, worker rights advocacy, and creating spaces for immigrant leadership and collective action.

Southern Movement for Multiracial Solidarity

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through multiracial organizing in Mississippi. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education, community organizing, and creating spaces for collective liberation in the South.
