Hearts on a Wire

A grassroots inside/outside collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through transgender prisoner support across Pennsylvania. They coordinate advocacy, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through gender self-determination and prison abolition organizing.

Let's Get Free

A mutual aid organization fostering solidarity and support for women and trans people in Pennsylvania prisons. They coordinate advocacy, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through prison abolition organizing and creating spaces for incarcerated voices.

Pennsylvania Institutional Law Project (PILP)

A legal advocacy organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through civil rights litigation for incarcerated people across Pennsylvania. They coordinate legal support, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through constitutional rights defense and creating systemic change in prisons and jails.

Commutation Now

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through advocacy for commutation and clemency in Pennsylvania. They coordinate support for incarcerated individuals seeking commutation, provide resources for families navigating the process, and build networks of solidarity while working towards transformative justice.

Landlord Watchlist Project

A community-driven initiative fostering mutual aid and housing justice through data transparency and tenant organizing in Pennsylvania. They coordinate documentation of landlord misconduct, maintain public databases, and build solidarity networks while empowering tenants with information and resources to fight housing exploitation.

Food Not Bombs Montgomery County

A mutual aid collective fostering food justice and community solidarity through free food sharing in Montgomery County. They coordinate food recovery, community meals, and solidarity networks while building power through direct action and creating spaces for food sovereignty.

Eastern PA Trans Equity Project

EPTEP provides direct services to transgender and gender-diverse Pennsylvanians in a thirty-three county region in Eastern PA.

Trans Minors Rights

Our goal is to empower transgender youth by educating our communities on issues facing young transgender people and providing youth with the knowledge and tools they need to combat gender dysphoria. Operating out of Lancaster.

TransFamily of NW Pennsylvania

TransFamily of NWPA is an organization created to serve the transgender community of Northwestern Pennsylvania serving Erie, Crawford, and Warren counties.
