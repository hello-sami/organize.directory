Statewide Organizations

  1. Wisconsin Labor for Palestine ↗ instagram.com

    A labor solidarity collective organizing mutual aid and support for Palestine through worker organizing and community mobilization across Wisconsin. They coordinate resources and build connections between labor movements and Palestine solidarity work.

  2. Organizing to Protect Education ↗ opewisconsin.org

    Organizing to Protect Education (OPE) is a workers' center dedicated to educating and organizing workers in educational settings in Wisconsin for the purposes of workplace support, mutual aid and protection of workers, students, and community members, and in defense of education as a social good.

  3. Worker Justice Wisconsin ↗ workerjustice.org

    Worker Justice Wisconsin is a community organization that empowers workers to ensure fair wages, safe working conditions, and a voice in the workplace through education, advocacy, and community organizing.

  4. WMF Wisconsin ↗ wiabortionfund.org

    Founded in 1972, WMF Wisconsin supports Wisconsin residents in accessing abortion care through direct funding assistance, education, and organizing. They work to remove financial and logistical barriers to abortion access while centering reproductive justice.

  5. Home To Stay ↗ hometostaywi.org

    A statewide resource network providing comprehensive support for returning citizens in Wisconsin. They connect people with resources across housing, employment, healthcare, legal aid, and other essential services while building community support networks for successful reentry.

  6. Fair Wisconsin ↗ fairwisconsin.com

    A statewide organization working to advance, achieve, and protect equality for LGBTQ+ Wisconsinites through education, grassroots organizing, civic engagement, and mutual aid. They build community power through legal advocacy, policy work, and resource sharing while maintaining an equality map of LGBTQ+ protections across Wisconsin.

  7. Wisconsin for Palestine ↗ instagram.com

    A statewide coalition organizing mutual aid and solidarity initiatives in support of Palestine across Wisconsin. They coordinate community resources, direct support actions, and educational programs while building connections between Wisconsin communities and Palestinian liberation movements through mutual aid networks, solidarity campaigns, and grassroots organizing.

  8. Voces de la Frontera ↗ vdlf.org

    Voces de la Frontera is Wisconsin's leading immigrant rights & low-wage workers center.
