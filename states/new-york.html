Prison Support

Prisoners' Legal Services of New York

A legal advocacy organization building power through mutual aid and solidarity with incarcerated people across New York State. They coordinate legal support, resource sharing, and advocacy networks while fighting for the rights and dignity of people in prison through direct representation and systemic change.

F2L Network

A mutual aid network providing direct support and resources to people facing state repression and incarceration across New York. They coordinate emergency financial assistance, commissary support, and solidarity networks while building power through prisoner advocacy and community care.

Statewide Initiatives

Community Voices Heard (CVH)

Member-led organization building power with low-income communities through grassroots organizing, mutual aid, and leadership development to advance racial, social, and economic justice across New York State.

Racial Justice

Twin Cities SURJ

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in the Twin Cities area. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
