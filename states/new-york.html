Prisoners' Legal Services of New York

A legal advocacy organization building power through mutual aid and solidarity with incarcerated people across New York State. They coordinate legal support, resource sharing, and advocacy networks while fighting for the rights and dignity of people in prison through direct representation and systemic change.

F2L Network

A mutual aid network providing direct support and resources to people facing state repression and incarceration across New York. They coordinate emergency financial assistance, commissary support, and solidarity networks while building power through prisoner advocacy and community care.

Community Voices Heard (CVH)

Member-led organization building power with low-income communities through grassroots organizing, mutual aid, and leadership development to advance racial, social, and economic justice across New York State.

New York Civil Liberties Union

A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and community power through civil rights advocacy and support across New York. They coordinate legal resources, community organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through policy advocacy, education, and creating spaces for civil liberties and justice.

New York Immigration Coalition

A statewide alliance fostering mutual aid and community power through immigrant rights advocacy and support across New York. They coordinate policy initiatives, community organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through immigrant justice, education access, and creating spaces for immigrant leadership.

New York Farmers Project

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through sustainable agriculture and food justice across New York. They coordinate resource sharing, farmer support, and solidarity networks while building power through food sovereignty, land access, and creating pathways for sustainable farming.
