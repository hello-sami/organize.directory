Statewide Initiatives

Anti-War Committee

A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and solidarity initiatives while organizing for peace and justice. They build community power through direct action, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while connecting various social movements and providing material support to communities in need.

DivestMN

A mutual aid and solidarity initiative organizing to divest Minnesota state funds from apartheid and occupation. They coordinate community resources, direct action campaigns, and solidarity networks while building grassroots power for economic justice and human rights.

Minnesota Mutual Aid Network

A network coordinating mutual aid efforts and connecting communities across Minnesota.

Twin Cities Mutual Aid

Supporting community-led mutual aid initiatives throughout the Minneapolis-St. Paul metropolitan area and beyond.

Minnesota Workers United

A worker-led organization building solidarity and mutual aid networks across Minnesota.

Rebuilding Together Minnesota

A mutual aid organization coordinating home repair and community revitalization initiatives across Minnesota since 1997. They organize direct support through their Safe at Home Program, Home Repair Program, and Community Revitalization Program while building networks of solidarity through volunteer mobilization and neighborhood preservation to ensure people can live independently and safely in their homes.

Education Justice Minnesota

A grassroots collective organizing mutual aid and solidarity initiatives focused on educational equity across Minnesota. They coordinate community resources, direct support, and advocacy while building networks of solidarity through student organizing, teacher support, and community education to ensure accessible and just education for all.

Documenting MN

A grassroots media collective documenting mutual aid and community organizing initiatives across Minnesota. They coordinate information sharing and documentation while building networks of solidarity through independent journalism, movement coverage, and community storytelling to amplify voices of resistance and social justice.

Sahan Journal

A nonprofit newsroom coordinating mutual aid through independent journalism focused on Minnesota's immigrants and communities of color. They organize information sharing and community storytelling while building networks of solidarity through in-depth reporting, community engagement, and amplifying voices often excluded from mainstream media.

Minnesota Tool Library

A mutual aid collective providing affordable access to tools and workshops across Minnesota. They coordinate resource sharing, skill building, and community education while building networks of solidarity through tool lending, DIY classes, and maker spaces to ensure everyone has access to the tools they need.

Minnesota Reformer

An independent nonprofit newsroom coordinating mutual aid through investigative journalism and community reporting. They organize information sharing and accountability while building networks of solidarity through in-depth coverage of power, policy, and their impacts on Minnesota communities.

BLCK Press

A social enterprise coordinating mutual aid through independent journalism focused on reconnecting news to Black culture in Minnesota. They organize community storytelling and media production while building networks of solidarity through Emmy-winning reporting, cultural preservation, and amplifying Black voices and experiences to foster trust and engagement across communities.

Revolutionary Emergency Partners (REP)

A mutual aid collective coordinating emergency response and community support initiatives across Minnesota. They organize direct assistance through street medic services, harm reduction supplies, and crisis response while building networks of solidarity through community health education, resource sharing, and collective care.

Black Visions

A Black-led organization coordinating mutual aid and community power building initiatives across Minnesota. They organize direct support, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building transformative justice through Black liberation organizing, political education, and healing justice practices.

New Justice Project

A Black-led organization coordinating mutual aid and community power building initiatives across Minnesota. They organize direct support through housing advocacy, climate justice programs, and community safety initiatives while building networks of solidarity through political education, leadership development, and organizing for structural equality for Black Minnesotans.

End Youth Prisons MN

A grassroots coalition coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives focused on ending youth incarceration in Minnesota. They organize direct support through advocacy, resource sharing, and community education while building networks of solidarity to create alternatives to youth detention and promote transformative justice approaches for young people.

Bdote Memory Map

A community-led initiative documenting and preserving Dakota sacred sites and cultural memory in Minnesota. They coordinate knowledge sharing and cultural preservation while building networks of solidarity through Indigenous storytelling, place-based education, and community-centered mapping to honor Dakota relationships with the land and water.

Makoce Ikikcupi (Land Recovery)

A Dakota land recovery and reparative justice project in Minisota Makoce (Minnesota). They coordinate the reestablishment of Dakota relationships with ancestral homeland through two community villages - Zani Otunwe (Village of Wellness) and Hohwoju Otunwe (Village of Vibrant Growth) - while building networks of solidarity through cultural revitalization, sustainable living practices, and Indigenous land stewardship.
