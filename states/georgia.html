Statewide Organizations

Amplify GA Collaborative

Coalition of reproductive health, rights, and justice organizations working to protect and expand abortion access across Georgia.

New Georgia Project

A grassroots organization building community power through civic engagement, mutual aid, and leadership development across Georgia. They coordinate voter registration, political education, and solidarity networks while centering Black, Brown, and young voters in the fight for a more equitable Georgia.

Asian American Advocacy Fund

A grassroots organization building power with Asian American communities through mutual aid, civic engagement, and community organizing across Georgia. They coordinate voter education, political advocacy, and solidarity networks while centering AAPI voices in the fight for progressive change.

Poder Unides

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support for immigrant and Latinx communities across Georgia. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for cultural connection and collective empowerment.

Georgia Latino Alliance for Human Rights (GLAHR)

educates, organizes and trains the Latino community in Georgia to defend and promote their civil and human rights.
