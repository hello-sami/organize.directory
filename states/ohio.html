Central Ohio Freedom Fund

A mutual aid collective providing bail support and post-release resources through community solidarity in Central Ohio. They coordinate bail fund distribution, court support, and solidarity networks while building power through prison abolition organizing and creating pathways for community-based support beyond incarceration.

Ohio Families Unite Against Police Brutality

A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through support for families affected by police violence across Ohio. They coordinate direct support initiatives, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through family support, community organizing, and creating spaces for healing and justice.

Ohio Families Unite PAC

A political action committee fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through advocacy and organizing for police accountability across Ohio. They coordinate direct support initiatives, legislative campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for systemic change.

SURJ Ohio

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing across Ohio. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Trans Ohio

TransOhio is dedicated to protecting and advancing the rights of trans, nonbinary, intersex, and gender nonconforming people in Ohio.

Black Transmen of Ohio

A safe, supportive and empowering home for transmen of color in Ohio.

Black Queer & Intersectional Collective

BQIC is a grassroots community organization in Central Ohio that works towards the liberation of Black LGBTQIA+ people from all walks of life through direct action, community organizing, education on our issues, and creating spaces to uplift our voices.

Trans Allies Ohio

We are a grassroots coalition of transgender youth, their families, and allies who advocate for equality for Ohio's trans and non-binary people.
