Statewide Organizations

  1. Carolina Abortion Fund ↗ carolinaabortionfund.org

    A mutual aid network providing financial, logistical, and emotional support to ensure abortion access for residents of North and South Carolina, while building power for reproductive justice through community organizing and advocacy.

  2. Kiran ↗ kiraninc.org

    A community-based organization providing crisis intervention, support services, and mutual aid to South Asian and Middle Eastern survivors of domestic violence across North Carolina. Through culturally-specific services, advocacy, and community organizing, they work to build safety and empowerment.

  3. NC Harm Reduction Coalition ↗ nchrc.org

    A statewide grassroots organization providing mutual aid and harm reduction services across North Carolina. Through direct support, resource sharing, and advocacy, they work to improve health outcomes and dignity for people impacted by drug use, incarceration, sex work, and HIV/hepatitis while building power for systemic change.

  4. NC Women's Prison Book Project ↗ ncwpb.org

    A volunteer-run collective providing mutual aid through books and letters to people incarcerated in women's prisons and jails across North Carolina. Through literature and correspondence, they work to break isolation, build connections, and support education and empowerment for incarcerated individuals.

  5. NC Tree ↗ nctree.xyz

    A mutual aid network organizing community support and resource sharing across North Carolina. Through their online platform and community organizing, they coordinate direct support initiatives, connect local mutual aid groups, and facilitate solidarity networks to address community needs throughout the state.

  6. Operation Stop CPS ↗ operationstopcps.com

    A grassroots organization providing mutual aid and advocacy support to families impacted by the child welfare system in North Carolina. They coordinate direct support, resource sharing, and community organizing while building networks of solidarity to support families, advocate for systemic change, and promote family preservation.

  7. Cypress Fund ↗ cypressfund.org

    A social justice fund providing mutual aid and resources to grassroots organizations across North Carolina. Through community-led grantmaking and donor organizing, they coordinate resource redistribution and direct support while building power for systemic change and community self-determination.

  8. Siembra NC ↗ siembranc.org

    A Latinx organization providing mutual aid and organizing support to immigrant communities across North Carolina. They coordinate direct support through wage theft recovery, workers' rights education, and rapid response networks while building community power through solidarity initiatives and collective action.

  9. Triangle Copwatch ↗ instagram.com

    A mutual aid collective organizing community defense and police accountability initiatives across North Carolina. They coordinate direct support, resource sharing, and community education while building networks of solidarity to protect communities and advocate for transformative justice solutions.

  10. Cooperate WNC ↗ cooperatewnc.org

    A cooperative development organization fostering mutual aid and economic democracy through worker-owned businesses and solidarity economy initiatives in Western North Carolina. They coordinate cooperative education, business development support, and solidarity networks while building power through collective ownership, community wealth building, and creating spaces for economic justice.

  11. ROAR (Rural Organizing and Resistance) ↗ instagram.com

    Hurricane Helene mutual aid hub based out of Western North Carolina.

  12. Holler Harm Reduction ↗ hollerharmreduction.org

    A free and confidential service that provides delivery of Harm Reduction supplies and overdose prevention materials to folks using drugs in Madison County, North Carolina.

  13. Down Home NC ↗ downhomenc.org

    Down Home North Carolina is building power with poor and working-class people in North Carolina's small towns and rural communities.

  14. Carolina Migrant Network ↗ carolinamigrantnetwork.org

    CMN offers free represent for people in NC/SC detention and removal proceedings.
