Statewide Initiatives

North Carolina Mutual Aid Network

A network coordinating mutual aid efforts and connecting communities across North Carolina.

Triangle Mutual Aid

Supporting community-led mutual aid initiatives throughout the Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill area.

Carolina Abortion Fund

A mutual aid network providing financial, logistical, and emotional support to ensure abortion access for residents of North and South Carolina, while building power for reproductive justice through community organizing and advocacy.

Kiran

A community-based organization providing crisis intervention, support services, and mutual aid to South Asian and Middle Eastern survivors of domestic violence across North Carolina. Through culturally-specific services, advocacy, and community organizing, they work to build safety and empowerment.

NC Harm Reduction Coalition

A statewide grassroots organization providing mutual aid and harm reduction services across North Carolina. Through direct support, resource sharing, and advocacy, they work to improve health outcomes and dignity for people impacted by drug use, incarceration, sex work, and HIV/hepatitis while building power for systemic change.

NC Women's Prison Book Project

A volunteer-run collective providing mutual aid through books and letters to people incarcerated in women's prisons and jails across North Carolina. Through literature and correspondence, they work to break isolation, build connections, and support education and empowerment for incarcerated individuals.
