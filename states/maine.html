The Good Life Center

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through sustainable living education and cultural preservation in Maine. They coordinate homesteading workshops, skill sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through ecological wisdom, simple living, and creating spaces for learning about self-reliant and sustainable lifestyles.
