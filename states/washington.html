Tenants Union of Washington State

Grassroots organization building collective power through education, outreach, and direct action to win housing justice and tenant rights through a racial equity lens.

Cascadia Food Program

A mutual aid collective organizing food distribution and community support across Washington state. They coordinate food rescue, resource sharing, and direct support while building sustainable networks of food access and community care throughout the Cascadia region.

Western WashCo for Racial Justice (WWRJ)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through racial and economic justice organizing in Western Washington County. Originally started as SURJ Forest Grove, they now work as part of the Rural Organizing Project family of human dignity groups, coordinating political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through multiracial organizing and community building.

SURJ Southwest Washington

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Southwest Washington. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.
