Statewide Organizations

  1. Illinois Coalition to End Permanent Punishments ↗ endpermanentpunishments.org

    A coalition working to end permanent punishments and support formerly incarcerated individuals through advocacy, mutual aid, and community organizing. They focus on policy reform, community education, and direct support for impacted individuals and families across Illinois.

  2. Illinois Coalition Against Domestic Violence ↗ ilcadv.org

    A statewide network fostering mutual aid and community power through domestic violence prevention and survivor support across Illinois. They coordinate direct assistance, shelter networks, and solidarity initiatives while building power through survivor-centered advocacy, community education, and creating pathways for safety and healing.

  3. Illinois Single Payer Coalition ↗ ilsinglepayer.org

    A grassroots coalition fostering mutual aid and community power through healthcare justice organizing across Illinois. They coordinate advocacy campaigns, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community education, policy change, and creating pathways for universal healthcare access.

  4. Transgender Justice Law Project ↗ tjlp.org

    We provide free, life and gender-affirming legal services to everyone on the gender spectrum regardless of financial status.

  5. Lift the Ban Coalition ↗ ltbcoalition.org

    A grassroots coalition fostering mutual aid and housing justice through organizing to end the state ban on rent control. They coordinate tenant organizing, policy advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through community education, direct action, and creating spaces for housing justice organizing across Illinois.

  6. Warehouse Workers For Justice ↗ ww4j.org

    Building power with warehouse workers to win safe, secure, high-paying jobs in Illinois' logistics industry.
