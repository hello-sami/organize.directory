Abortion Access

Yellowhammer Fund

A reproductive justice organization fostering mutual aid and healthcare access through abortion funding and support in Alabama and Mississippi. They coordinate financial assistance, practical support, and solidarity networks while building power through reproductive justice education, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for bodily autonomy and community care. Their work includes funding abortion care, providing emergency contraception, and supporting people across the Deep South in accessing reproductive healthcare.

Housing Justice

LIHC Alabama

A housing justice organization fostering mutual aid and community support through advocacy and direct assistance for low-income housing residents in Alabama. They work to build tenant power, prevent evictions, and ensure safe, affordable housing through community organizing and solidarity networks.

Immigrant Solidarity

Alabama Coalition for Immigrant Justice (ACIJ)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and immigrant rights through community organizing and direct support across Alabama. They coordinate legal assistance, emergency response networks, and solidarity initiatives while building power through immigrant-led organizing, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for immigrant community empowerment and collective action.

Prison Abolition

Project Hope to Abolish the Death Penalty (PHADP)

A prisoner-founded and prisoner-led organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through death penalty abolition work in Alabama. They coordinate advocacy campaigns, resource sharing, and support networks while building power through education initiatives, direct support for death row prisoners and their families, and creating spaces for transformative justice.

Alabama Justice Initiative

A grassroots organization working to end mass incarceration in Alabama through mutual aid, community organizing, and advocacy. They provide direct support to incarcerated individuals and their families while building community power for systemic change.

Justice for Lakeith Smith

A mutual aid campaign providing support and resources while advocating for justice for Lakeith Smith and other youth affected by Alabama's accomplice liability law.

Alabama Books to Prisons Project

A mutual aid initiative providing books and educational materials to incarcerated individuals across Alabama, while also facilitating penpal connections and publishing an Inside Out Newsletter. They work to reduce isolation in prison life and increase access to educational resources.

Communities Not Prisons

A coalition of directly impacted people, communities, and organizations working to stop prison construction in Alabama through mutual aid, community organizing, and advocacy. They focus on supporting communities affected by mass incarceration while envisioning alternatives to prison expansion.

International Solidarity

Global Solidarity Alabama

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and international solidarity through community organizing and direct support across Alabama. They coordinate solidarity initiatives, political education, and support networks while building power through collective action, cross-border solidarity, and creating spaces for global justice movements.

Labor Rights

ALTRAC Works

A worker-led organization fostering mutual aid and labor solidarity through organizing and direct support across Alabama. They coordinate worker advocacy, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through collective action, worker education, and creating spaces for labor organizing and mutual support.

Queer and Trans Solidarity

Alabama Queer Resistance

A mutual aid collective providing support, resources, and community organizing for LGBTQ+ individuals across Alabama, with a focus on direct support and building queer community power.

Statewide Initiatives

Hometown Action

A multi-racial, working-class organization building mutual aid networks and community power in small-town and rural Alabama communities, with a focus on racial, gender, economic, and climate justice.

¡HICA! (Hispanic Interest Coalition of Alabama)

A community development and advocacy organization that champions economic equality, civic engagement, and social justice for Latino and immigrant families in Alabama through mutual aid, direct support services, and community empowerment programs.

Alabama Appleseed

A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and justice through research, advocacy, and community organizing across Alabama. They coordinate legal support, policy reform initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through systemic change advocacy, particularly focusing on criminal justice reform, economic justice, and educational equity.

Disability Justice

New Disabled South

A regional organization fostering mutual aid and disability justice through community support and solidarity across the American South. They coordinate resource sharing, accessibility initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through disability justice organizing, community care, and creating spaces for disabled community empowerment.
