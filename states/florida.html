Florida Immigrant Coalition (FLIC)

A statewide alliance of immigrant rights organizations providing mutual aid, legal support, and community organizing. Through their network of grassroots groups, they offer direct assistance to immigrant families while building power for systemic change through advocacy and collective action.

Solidarity SFL

A grassroots organization building mutual aid networks and community power through direct action and solidarity initiatives across South Florida.

Change Comes Now

Grassroots organization building mutual aid networks and support systems for individuals and families impacted by the criminal legal system in Florida through advocacy, education, and direct assistance.

SURJ Florida

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing across Florida. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

FL Harm Redux

A grassroots harm reduction organization providing mutual aid and support services across Florida. They coordinate harm reduction supplies, education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for health justice and collective care.

Coalition of Immokalee Workers

Worker-based human rights organization internationally recognized for its achievements in fighting human trafficking and gender-based violence at work. Operating in Immokalee, FL.

Florida Palestine Network

Florida Palestine Network is a Palestinian woman founded and led organization struggling for the liberation of the Palestinian people and oppressed people everywhere.
