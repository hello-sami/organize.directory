Racial Justice

La Plata SURJ

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in the Four Corners region. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with Southwest Movement for Black Lives.

Food Security

Durango Food Not Bombs

A grassroots mutual aid collective dedicated to recovering and sharing free vegan meals in Durango. They coordinate food recovery, community meals, and resource distribution while building solidarity through direct action against hunger and food waste.

Peace & Justice

Durango Peace and Justice

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through peace and justice organizing in Durango and the Four Corners region. They coordinate direct action support, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective resistance.
