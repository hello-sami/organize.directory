Alternatives for Community and Environment (ACE)

A grassroots organization building community power through mutual aid and environmental justice initiatives across Massachusetts. They coordinate direct support, resource sharing, and community organizing while advocating for environmental justice, public health, and sustainable communities.

Queer & Trans Mutual Aid Massachusetts (QTMAMA)

A collective of LGBTQIA+ residents coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives across Massachusetts. They organize direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building resilient queer communities through neighborhood organizing and collective action.

Food For Free

A mutual aid organization coordinating food rescue and distribution initiatives across Eastern Massachusetts. They organize direct assistance through food rescue, home delivery, school markets, and meal programs while building sustainable networks of food access and community care, serving over 130,000 neighbors annually.

Lawrence Community Fridge

A community-run mutual aid project providing free food access through a public refrigerator in Lawrence. Located outside of 163 East Haverhill Street, they coordinate food distribution, resource sharing, and community care while building power through neighborhood solidarity and creating accessible spaces for food security.

Stone Soup Cafe

A pay-what-you-can community cafe fostering mutual aid and food security through shared meals and community building in Massachusetts. They coordinate food access programs, community gatherings, and solidarity networks while building power through food justice, collective care, and creating spaces for nourishment and connection.

Massachussetts Transgender Political Coalition

MTPC works to ensure the wellbeing, safety, and lived equity of all trans, nonbinary, and gender expansive community members in Massachusetts.
