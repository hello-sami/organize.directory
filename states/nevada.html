Statewide Organizations

Mass Liberation Project

Grassroots organization building power with communities impacted by mass incarceration through mutual aid, political education, and organizing campaigns to transform Nevada's criminal legal system.

Wild West Fund

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and reproductive justice through abortion access and support across Nevada. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through healthcare access, community organizing, and creating spaces for reproductive autonomy.

Northern Nevada Harm Reduction Alliance

Organization expanding harm reduction education, overdose prevention, drug trend surveillance and notification, soft tissue infection prevention, outreach, and linkage to services.
