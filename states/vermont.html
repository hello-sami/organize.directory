Migrant Justice

A farmworker-led organization building power through mutual aid, organizing, and advocacy in Vermont's dairy industry. Through initiatives like the Milk with Dignity program, they work to ensure dignified housing, fair wages, and safe working conditions while providing direct support to migrant workers.

Waterbury Anti-Racism Coalition

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through anti-racist organizing in Waterbury and surrounding communities. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and community organizing.

Vermonters for Justice in Palestine

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through education, advocacy, and direct action in support of Palestinian human rights. They coordinate political education, solidarity networks, and community organizing while building power through human rights advocacy and creating spaces for movement building across Vermont.

The Root Social Justice Center

A community organization centering BIPOC experiences and providing mutual aid through various programs including their BIPOC Mutual Aid Support Network, free expungement clinics, and community workshops. They focus on building racial justice movements while offering direct support through accessible programming, skill-sharing, and resource distribution to BIPOC communities across Vermont.

Out in The Open

Out in the Open connects rural LGBTQ+ people to build community, visibility, knowledge and power. We are a multiracial, majority working class, grassroots, movement and capacity building organization based in Wabanaki territory throughout the states of Vermont and Maine.

Vermont Mutual Aid

A grassroots network fostering community solidarity and direct support across Vermont through mutual aid organizing. They coordinate resource sharing, emergency assistance, and community care while building networks of support through local organizing and collective action.
