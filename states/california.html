Rural Organizing

Rural SURJ of Northern California

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in rural Northern California. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations in rural communities.

Indigenous Solidarity

Tongva Land

A mutual aid initiative fostering solidarity and support for Tongva people, the original inhabitants of the Los Angeles Basin and Southern Channel Islands. They coordinate land tax contributions, cultural preservation, and solidarity networks while building power through Indigenous sovereignty, land stewardship, and creating spaces for Tongva cultural revitalization.

Statewide Initiatives

Líderes Campesinas

A grassroots organization led by and for farmworker women, providing mutual aid, leadership development, and organizing support while advocating for workers' rights, gender justice, and environmental health in agricultural communities across California.

California Mutual Aid Network

A statewide network connecting mutual aid groups and resources across California.

Ground Game LA Mutual Aid Network

Coordinating mutual aid efforts and connecting communities across Southern California.

Communities for a Better Environment (CBE)

Environmental justice organization building power in working-class communities of color through grassroots organizing, mutual aid, and community-led solutions to environmental racism in Northern and Southern California.

Transformative Programming Works

Building mutual aid networks and support systems for formerly incarcerated people through community organizing, education, and solidarity initiatives.

Asian Prisoner Support Committee - ROOTS Program

Supporting incarcerated Asian, Pacific Islander, and ally communities through ethnic studies education, book programs, correspondence initiatives, and reentry planning across California prisons.

Justice Teams Network

A statewide network coordinating rapid response, mutual aid, and community support for survivors of state violence. They organize direct support through crisis response teams, healing justice programs, and solidarity networks while building community-based alternatives to policing across California.

NorCal Resist

A grassroots organization coordinating mutual aid and community support initiatives across Northern California. They organize direct assistance through food distribution, deportation defense, asylum support, and climate resilience programs while building networks of solidarity through their Seeds of Solidarity farm, brake light repair clinics, and community accompaniment services.

Power Blossoms

A mutual aid collective fostering solidarity and support for LGBTQ+ people in prison across California. They coordinate letter writing, resource sharing, and care packages while building connections through pen pal programs and advocacy for queer and trans prisoners.

Dare to Struggle

A revolutionary organization building power through mutual aid, political education, and direct action to advance the struggle for liberation and social justice.
