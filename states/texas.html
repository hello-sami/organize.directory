Statewide Organizations

Texas Civil Rights Project

A legal advocacy organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through civil rights litigation and community organizing across Texas. They coordinate legal support, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through constitutional rights defense and creating systemic change.

La Union del Pueblo Entero (LUPE)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through immigrant and worker community organizing across Texas. They coordinate direct support, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through community leadership development and creating spaces for collective action.

TX Abolitionists

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through prison abolition organizing across Texas. They coordinate direct support, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through community education, direct action, and creating spaces for abolition movement building.

Harm Reduction TX

A grassroots collective providing harm reduction services and mutual aid support across Texas. They coordinate direct support, supply distribution, and community care while building power through harm reduction education, peer outreach, and creating spaces for health justice and community wellbeing.

Trans Texas

A trans-led mutual aid network providing direct support and solidarity for transgender and gender non-conforming communities across Texas. They coordinate emergency assistance, resource distribution, and community care initiatives while building power through trans liberation organizing and collective action.

Estok' Gna

An Indigenous-led collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through cultural preservation and land defense in Texas. They coordinate community support, traditional knowledge sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through Indigenous sovereignty and creating pathways for cultural resurgence and land reclamation.

Texas for Sudan

Texas for Sudan, dedicated to amplifying Sudanese voices and participation in events centered around Sudanese liberation efforts in Texas.

Texas Equal Access Fund

We're here to help Texans access abortion care by helping them pay for it.

Jane's Due Process

We help TX teens get abortions & birth control.

Woori Juntos

Grassroots-based organization empowering impacted Asian and immigrant Texans through culturally competent service, organizing, & advocacy

Faith in TX

Faith in TX organizes and builds power with "the people" so that we "the people" will have greater access to the power to govern our shared society.
