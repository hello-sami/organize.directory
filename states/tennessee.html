Statewide Initiatives

The STAY Project

A youth-led organization building power through mutual aid, leadership development, and organizing across Central Appalachia. STAY works to create sustainable and inclusive communities while supporting young people fighting for social, economic, and environmental justice.

Tennessee Immigrant & Refugee Rights Coalition (TIRRC)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through immigrant and refugee community organizing across Tennessee. They coordinate direct support, advocacy campaigns, and solidarity networks while building power through immigrant leadership development and creating spaces for community care.

Racial Justice

SURJ Blount County

A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Blount County. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

Education & Movement Building

Highlander Research and Education Center

A historic grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and movement building through popular education and organizing in Tennessee. They coordinate leadership development, cultural work, and solidarity networks while building power through intergenerational organizing, political education, and creating spaces for social justice movements across the South and Appalachia.
