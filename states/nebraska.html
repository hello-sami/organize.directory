Statewide Initiatives

DSA Nebraska

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through democratic socialist organizing across Nebraska. They coordinate direct support, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through collective organizing and community care.

OutNebraska

A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and community power through LGBTQ+ advocacy and support across Nebraska. They coordinate resource sharing, community organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through policy advocacy, education, and creating spaces for queer and trans liberation.

Nebraska Appleseed

A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and community power through legal advocacy and grassroots organizing across Nebraska. They coordinate policy initiatives, community organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through economic justice, healthcare access, immigrant rights, and creating systemic change for all Nebraskans.

Nebraska AIDS Project

A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and community care through HIV/AIDS support services and sexual health advocacy across Nebraska. They coordinate HIV testing, PrEP access, case management, and solidarity networks while building power through education, healthcare access, and creating spaces for people impacted by HIV/AIDS to thrive.

Norfolk Area Pride

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support through LGBTQ+ advocacy and services in Northeast Nebraska. They coordinate community education, support networks, and solidarity initiatives while building power through LGBTQ+ visibility, safety awareness, and creating spaces for queer and trans people to thrive in rural Nebraska.
