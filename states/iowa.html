Iowa Citizens for Community Improvement (CCI)

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through statewide organizing and advocacy in Iowa. They coordinate direct action, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through racial, economic, and environmental justice campaigns.

Iowa Mutual Aid Network

The Iowa Mutual Aid Network is made up of numerous individuals, collectives, and affinity groups working together and along side each other to change the material conditions of oppressed communities.

Iowa Trans Mutual Aid Fund

The Iowa Trans Mutual Aid Fund financially supports Trans, Nonbinary, and Gender Diverse Iowans as they access gender-affirming care.

Prarielands Freedom Fund

Paying immigration bond and protester bail for family, dignity, and freedom.

Iowa Abortion Access Fund

Committed to providing access to abortion services for folks in Iowa, including the QCA, who lack resources to pay.
