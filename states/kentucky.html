Statewide Organizations

  1. The STAY Project ↗ thestayproject.net

    A youth-led organization building power through mutual aid, leadership development, and organizing across Central Appalachia. STAY works to create sustainable and inclusive communities while supporting young people fighting for social, economic, and environmental justice.

  2. Kentuckians For The Commonwealth (KFTC) ↗ kftc.org

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through statewide organizing and advocacy in Kentucky. They coordinate direct action, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through environmental justice, economic justice, and creating spaces for democratic participation and social change.

  3. Kentucky Health Justice Network ↗ kentuckyhealthjusticenetwork.org

    Funding abortion and gender-affirming care in Kentucky.

  4. Kentucky Legal Aid ↗ klaid.org

    Kentucky Legal Aid is the only free, civil legal services provider in South-Western Kentucky.
