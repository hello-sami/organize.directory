  1. Dare to Struggle New Mexico ↗ instagram.com

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through direct action and political education across New Mexico. They coordinate community support initiatives, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through collective organizing and creating spaces for social justice.

  2. Tewa Women United ↗ tewawomenunited.org

    An Indigenous women-led organization fostering mutual aid and community power through cultural preservation and environmental justice in Northern New Mexico. They coordinate resource sharing, land-based education, and solidarity networks while building power through Indigenous sovereignty, reproductive justice, and creating spaces for healing and transformation.

  3. New Mexico Immigrant Law Center ↗ nmilc.org

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community power through immigrant rights advocacy and support across New Mexico. They coordinate legal resources, community organizing, and solidarity networks while building power through policy advocacy, education, and creating spaces for immigrant justice.

  4. New Mexico Indigenous Youth Council ↗ nmindigenousyouth.org

    A youth-led organization fostering mutual aid and community power through Indigenous youth organizing and cultural revitalization. They coordinate resource sharing, leadership development, and solidarity networks while building power through cultural education, environmental justice, and creating spaces for Indigenous youth to thrive.
