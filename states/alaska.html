Native Movement

An Indigenous-led organization building grassroots power through decolonial education, mutual aid, and community organizing across Alaska. They focus on climate justice, food sovereignty, Indigenous rights, and cultural revitalization while supporting community-led solutions and traditional knowledge systems.

Identity Alaska

A statewide LGBTQ+ organization providing mutual aid, community support, and advocacy for Alaska's queer and trans communities. They coordinate resource sharing, peer support networks, and direct assistance while building community power through education, organizing, and creating safe spaces for connection and solidarity.

Just Transition Alaska

A grassroots coalition fostering mutual aid and climate justice through community-led initiatives across Alaska. They coordinate climate action, indigenous solidarity, and just transition programs while building power through collective organizing, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for environmental justice and sustainable futures.
