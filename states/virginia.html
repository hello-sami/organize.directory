Statewide Organizations

  1. Blue Ridge Community Bail Fund ↗ blueridgebailfund.org

    A volunteer-run mutual aid organization providing bail assistance to people held pre-trial in the Shenandoah Valley region, including Staunton, Harrisonburg, Page County, and Charlottesville. Through their revolving bail fund, they work to ensure that people do not remain incarcerated simply due to inability to pay, while addressing the disproportionate impact of cash bail on communities of color and low-income individuals.

  2. Power Safe Place Resource Center of Virginia ↗ powersafeplace.org

    Power Safe Place Resource Center currently operates in Rural Virginia. We have been providing financial support, job readiness training, transportation, clothes, food, shoes, condoms and more for the LGBTQIA community in Virginia.

  3. Transgender Assistance Program of Virginia ↗ instagram.com

    A 501(c)3 non-profit organization by trans people, for trans people. Dedicated to ending trans homelessness across the Commonwealth of VA.

  4. SURJ Harrisonburg-Timberville ↗ surj-ht.wixsite.com

    A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in the Harrisonburg-Timberville area. They coordinate community organizing, mobilizing, and education initiatives while building power through political education, direct action support, and creating spaces for accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

  5. SURJ Northern Virginia ↗ surjnova.org

    A chapter of Showing Up for Racial Justice fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through white anti-racist organizing in Northern Virginia. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through racial justice education and accountable organizing in partnership with BIPOC-led organizations.

  6. The 40 Strong ↗ the40strong.com

    Collective of incarcerated and formerly incarcerated people organizing for prison abolition and supporting families affected by incarceration in Virginia.

  7. Blue Ridge Abortion Fund ↗ blueridgeabortionfund.org

    A community-based mutual aid organization providing financial support, transportation, and lodging assistance for abortion care since 1989. Through their funding network and support services, they work to reduce barriers to access while building community care networks and offering comprehensive support for those seeking abortion care across Virginia.

  8. Beloved Community Incubator ↗ belovedcommunityincubator.org

    A community-based organization fostering mutual aid and economic justice through cooperative development in the DMV region. They coordinate worker-owned business incubation, solidarity economy initiatives, and community wealth building while creating spaces for economic democracy, racial equity, and collective liberation through their various programs and support networks.

  9. Care Collective of SWVA ↗ carecollectiveofswva.wordpress.com

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support in Southwest Virginia. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating spaces for collective care and support in the region.

  10. The Green Cab ↗ thegreencab.org

    Our mission is to provide a range of practical and logistical support options for people seeking abortion care in Virginia.

  11. Rainy Day Fund ↗ instagram.com

    A mutual aid network providing direct financial assistance and support to people in need across South Carolina and Virginia. They coordinate emergency financial aid, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community care and creating pathways for collective support.
