Statewide Initiatives

New Jersey Mutual Aid Network

A network coordinating mutual aid efforts and connecting communities across New Jersey.

North Jersey Mutual Aid

Supporting community-led mutual aid initiatives throughout North Jersey and surrounding areas.

NJ Trans Queer Defense

A mutual aid collective organizing direct support and community defense for trans and queer people across New Jersey. They coordinate resource sharing, solidarity networks, and community care while building power through direct action, peer support, and creating affirming spaces for trans and queer liberation.

Say Gay NJ

A grassroots mutual aid collective organizing support and community defense for LGBTQ+ youth across New Jersey. They coordinate resource sharing, solidarity networks, and direct assistance while building community power through youth organizing, education advocacy, and creating affirming spaces for queer and trans youth.

Books Behind Bars

A volunteer-run prison abolition project providing free books to people incarcerated in New Jersey. They coordinate direct support through book requests and distribution while building networks of solidarity through literacy access, educational empowerment, and challenging mass incarceration. Based in Wildwood, they are the first and only books-to-prison program in New Jersey.

New Jersey Harm Reduction Coalition

A mutual aid network providing free harm reduction supplies and support across New Jersey. They coordinate direct assistance through naloxone distribution, syringe access, and safer use supplies while building community power through evidence-informed public health resources, education, and advocacy to promote the safety and dignity of people who use drugs.

NJ Peace and Justice Works

A grassroots organization building mutual aid networks and solidarity through peace and justice initiatives across New Jersey. They coordinate direct support, community organizing, and resource sharing while building power through education and collective action.
