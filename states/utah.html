Utah Valley Mutual Aid

Utah Valley Mutual Aid is a mutual aid network for the whole Utah Valley. We seek to empower each other individually and communally through direct action and mutual aid. This might be groceries, medical supplies, prescriptions or other necessities that are hard to acquire in times of panic.

Awareness into action. Solidarity not charity.
