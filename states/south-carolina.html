Statewide Organizations

  1. Harriet Hancock Center ↗ harriethancockcenter.org

    A statewide organization fostering mutual aid and community support for LGBTQ+ people across South Carolina. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing, support services, and creating affirming spaces for queer and trans people to connect and thrive.

  2. The Ladies Room ↗ instagram.com

    A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and community support for LGBTQ+ people across South Carolina. They coordinate direct assistance, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating affirming spaces for queer and trans people to connect and thrive.

  3. Carolina Abortion Fund ↗ carolinaabortionfund.org

    A mutual aid network providing financial, logistical, and emotional support to ensure abortion access for residents of North and South Carolina, while building power for reproductive justice through community organizing and advocacy.

  4. Cypress Fund ↗ cypressfund.org

    A social justice fund providing mutual aid and resources to grassroots organizations across South Carolina. Through community-led grantmaking and donor organizing, they coordinate resource redistribution and direct support while building power for systemic change and community self-determination.

  5. Palmetto State Abortion Fund ↗ instagram.com

    A grassroots organization providing mutual aid and support for abortion access across South Carolina. They coordinate direct financial assistance, practical support, and solidarity networks while building power through reproductive justice organizing and creating pathways for healthcare access and bodily autonomy.

  6. Rainy Day Fund ↗ instagram.com

    A mutual aid network providing direct financial assistance and support to people in need across South Carolina and Virginia. They coordinate emergency financial aid, resource sharing, and solidarity networks while building power through community care and creating pathways for collective support.

  7. SC Housing Justice Network ↗ instagram.com

    A grassroots network fostering mutual aid and community support for housing justice across South Carolina. They coordinate tenant organizing, eviction defense, and solidarity networks while building power through community organizing and creating pathways for housing justice and tenant rights.

  8. SC Death Penalty Action Network ↗ instagram.com

    A grassroots organization working to abolish the death penalty in South Carolina through mutual aid, advocacy, and community organizing. They coordinate direct support for death row prisoners and their families, education initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through abolition organizing and creating pathways for justice transformation.

  9. Carolina Migrant Network ↗ carolinamigrantnetwork.org

    CMN offers free represent for people in NC/SC detention and removal proceedings.
