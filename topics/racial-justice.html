Racial Justice Resources & Support

10 groups

Organizations and resources focused on racial equity, anti-racism, and supporting communities of color through mutual aid, solidarity, and collective action.

National Organizations

  1. Spirit of Mandela ↗ spiritofmandela.org

    A national coalition fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through international human rights advocacy and organizing. They coordinate direct support initiatives, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through movement building, international tribunal organizing, and creating spaces for addressing human rights violations against Black, Brown, and Indigenous peoples.

  2. Showing Up for Racial Justice (SURJ) ↗ surj.org

    A national network organizing white people for racial justice through mutual aid and community solidarity. They coordinate education initiatives, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through political education, community organizing, and creating spaces for accountable allyship across the country.

  3. Catalyst Project ↗ collectiveliberation.org

    A national organization fostering mutual aid and collective liberation through anti-racist education and organizing. They coordinate the Anne Braden Program, political education workshops, and solidarity networks while building power through movement building, leadership development, and creating spaces for white activists to become accountable anti-racist organizers.

  4. White Coats 4 Black Lives ↗ whitecoats4blacklives.org

    A national organization of medical students fostering mutual aid and racial justice through organizing in healthcare. They coordinate direct action support, medical education reform initiatives, and solidarity networks while building power through anti-racist organizing in medical institutions and creating spaces for health justice.

  5. Black Alliance for Peace ↗ blackallianceforpeace.com

    A national organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through anti-war and anti-imperialist organizing. They coordinate political education, direct action support, and solidarity networks while building power through movement building, international solidarity, and creating spaces for Black-led anti-war organizing and peace initiatives.

  6. Presente.org ↗ presente.org

    A national Latinx organization fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through racial justice organizing and digital advocacy. They coordinate direct action campaigns, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through movement building, digital organizing, and creating spaces for Latinx-led social justice initiatives.

  7. American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee (ADC) ↗ adc.org

    A civil rights organization fostering mutual aid and community power through Arab-American organizing and advocacy. They coordinate legal support, cultural programming, and solidarity networks while building power through civil rights advocacy, political education, and creating spaces for Arab-American community empowerment.

  8. National Alliance Against Racist & Political Repression (NAARPR) ↗ naarpr.org

    Founded in 1973 in Chicago to mount organized action against unjust treatment of individuals because of race or political beliefs. Has multiple chapters across the US.

  9. BYP100 ↗ byp100.org

    BYP100 is National, member-based organization of Black 18-35 year old activists and organizers, dedicated to creating justice and freedom for all Black people. We do this through building a network focused on transformative leadership development, direct action organizing, advocacy, and political education using a Black queer feminist lens.

  10. Turpin Cares ↗ turpincares.org

    We distribute food, hygiene products, and relief items to people in need on Chicago's south side.
