Stop Cop City Resources & Support

Primary Organizing & Resources

Stop Cop City Network

A coalition of community members and organizations working to prevent the construction of a militarized police training facility in Atlanta's South River Forest. Through direct action, mutual aid, and community organizing, they advocate for environmental justice and community-centered alternatives to policing.

Defend the Atlanta Forest

A decentralized movement dedicated to protecting the Atlanta Forest and stopping the construction of Cop City. They coordinate mutual aid efforts, forest defense actions, and educational initiatives about the environmental and social impact of the proposed facility.

Legal Support & Solidarity

Atlanta Solidarity Fund

Provides support for people arrested for participating in social movements, including those protesting Cop City. Their mutual aid efforts include bail funds, legal assistance, and court support for activists and community defenders.

National Lawyers Guild Mass Defense

Offers legal support for protesters and activists, including those involved in the Stop Cop City movement. Through mutual aid organizing, they provide legal observers, know-your-rights trainings, and coordinate defense for those facing charges.

Environmental Justice & Land Defense

Sierra Club Georgia Chapter

Supports efforts to protect the South River Forest and oppose Cop City. Their mutual aid work includes advocacy, public education about the ecological importance of the forest, and building coalitions between environmental and social justice movements.

South River Watershed Alliance

Works to protect the South River watershed, which includes the forest where Cop City is planned. Through community solidarity and environmental advocacy, they highlight the ecological impact of the proposed development and support grassroots resistance efforts.
