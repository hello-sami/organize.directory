Urban Displacement Project

A research initiative fostering mutual aid and housing justice through critical research and community partnerships. They coordinate research on gentrification and displacement, develop policy tools, and build solidarity networks while creating accessible resources for communities fighting displacement and advocating for housing justice.

Renters Rising

Renters Rising is a national alliance of renters working to shift the balance of power between renters and corporate landlords to guarantee that renters are able to live with dignity.
