Housing Justice & Tenant Rights

Organizations and resources working to advance tenant rights, affordable housing, and community-controlled housing solutions through collective action and solidarity.

Tenant Rights Organizations

  1. Tenants Together ↗ tenantstogether.org

    Statewide coalition of local tenant organizations dedicated to defending and advancing the rights of California tenants through education, organizing, and advocacy.

  2. Right to Counsel NYC ↗ righttocounselnyc.org

    Coalition that won the first right to counsel for tenants facing eviction in the nation, ensuring legal representation for low-income tenants in housing court.

  3. Autonomous Tenants Union Network ↗ atun-rsia.org

    A North American collaborative of tenant unions committed to building tenant power through direct action, mutual support, and democratic organizing principles.

  4. Renters Rising ↗ renters-rising.org

    A national alliance of renters working to shift the balance of power between renters and corporate landlords to guarantee that renters are able to live with dignity and security in their homes.

Community-Controlled Housing Initiatives

  1. Housing Not Profit ↗ housingnotprofit.org

    Coalition working to keep housing in community hands through community land trusts and housing cooperatives, building alternatives to the for-profit housing model.

Research & Resources

  1. Urban Displacement Project ↗ urbandisplacement.org

    A research initiative fostering mutual aid and housing justice through critical research and community partnerships. They coordinate research on gentrification and displacement, develop policy tools, and build solidarity networks while creating accessible resources for communities fighting displacement and advocating for housing justice.
