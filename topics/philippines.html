Philippines

National Initiatives

BAYAN-USA

An alliance of progressive Filipino organizations in the U.S. representing the national democratic movement in the Philippines, working on issues of human rights, anti-imperialism, and genuine democracy.

GABRIELA USA

A grassroots organization advocating for the rights and welfare of Filipino women through education, organizing, and mobilizing for gender justice, economic justice, and human rights in the Philippines and the U.S.

Anakbayan-USA

A comprehensive national Filipino youth and student organization that addresses issues affecting Filipinos in the U.S. and the Philippines through community organizing, education, and direct action.

Migrante International

A global alliance of Filipino migrant organizations focused on protecting the rights and welfare of overseas Filipino workers and their families through advocacy, education, and direct support services.

National Alliance for Filipino Concerns (NAFCON)

A national multi-issue alliance of Filipino organizations and individuals in the United States serving Filipino communities, advocating for social, economic, and racial justice, and advancing the rights and welfare of Filipinos.

Malaya Movement USA

A movement of Filipinos and allies in the U.S. working to defend democracy, human rights, and sovereignty in the Philippines through education, advocacy, and mobilization.

Kursong Binhi

A collective offering political education and organizing training for Filipinos in the diaspora, focusing on sustainable activism, community building, and addressing root causes of issues affecting Filipino communities.

Filipino Solidarity Centre

An organization fostering solidarity between Filipinos globally and their allies, providing resources for mutual aid, political education, and coordinating solidarity actions for Philippine issues.

Pesante-USA

An organization supporting Filipino peasants and agricultural workers through solidarity campaigns, education about land rights, and advocacy for agrarian reform in the Philippines.

Philippine Forum

A community-based organization providing services to the Filipino migrant community while organizing for immigrant rights, workers' rights, and social justice through community empowerment programs.

Kamalayan Collective

A grassroots organization building capacity among Filipino youth through cultural work, arts-based organizing, and political education connecting issues in Filipino communities across the diaspora.

Kasama

An organization promoting mutual aid, community empowerment, and political education for Filipino communities in the U.S., organizing around issues of labor rights, immigration, and anti-imperialism.

BABAE

A Filipino women's organization focused on addressing issues of gender-based violence, trafficking, and discrimination against Filipino women through advocacy, education, and support services.

Kabataan Alliance

A national alliance of Filipino youth and student organizations in the United States advocating for education rights, immigrant rights, and international solidarity through grassroots organizing and leadership development.

Filipinos for Justice

the vision of FAJ is to foster a Filipino community with the power to advance social & economic justice.

Filipino Solidarity Network

A network coordinating solidarity campaigns and mutual aid initiatives in support of human rights, environmental justice, and indigenous rights in the Philippines through global advocacy and direct action.

Migrante USA

An alliance of Filipino migrant and worker organizations in the U.S.
