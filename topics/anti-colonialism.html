Anti-Colonialism Initiatives

Community Peacemaker Teams (CPT)

An international organization building partnerships to transform violence and oppression through mutual aid and direct action. They work alongside communities resisting colonialism in Palestine, Iraqi Kurdistan, Colombia, and Turtle Island (North America), providing support through nonviolent intervention, documentation, and solidarity networks.

Behind Enemy Lines

Anti-Imperialist political education and action organization, primarily based in NYC and Chicago. They organize against colonialism and imperialism through community education, direct action, and solidarity work with colonized peoples.

Nodutdol

An organization of diasporic Koreans and comrades based in the United States and Canada, organizing for a world free of imperialism, and for Korea's reunification and national liberation. They work on anti-war initiatives, solidarity campaigns, and community education about Korean liberation struggles and US militarism in Korea.
