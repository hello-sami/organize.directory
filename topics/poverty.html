Organizations and initiatives focused on combating economic inequality, debt resistance, and building economic alternatives through mutual aid and collective action.

Debt Resistance

Debt Collective

A debtors' union fostering mutual aid and economic justice through collective organizing and direct action. They coordinate debt strikes, financial education, and solidarity networks while building power through collective bargaining, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for debtors to fight predatory financial practices and build economic alternatives.
