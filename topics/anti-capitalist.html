Anti-Capitalist Organizations & Resources

4 groups

  1. Union for Radical Political Economics (URPE) ↗ urpe.org

    An interdisciplinary association fostering critical analysis and mutual aid through radical economic education since 1968. They coordinate research, publications, and conferences while building networks of solidarity through heterodox economics, Marxian political economy, and creating spaces for developing alternatives to mainstream economic theory and capitalist social relations.

  2. Counter Currents ↗ countercurrents.org

    An independent news platform fostering critical analysis and mutual aid through radical journalism and alternative perspectives. They coordinate investigative reporting, political analysis, and solidarity networks while building power through independent media, grassroots journalism, and creating spaces for marginalized voices and anti-capitalist discourse.

  3. Green and Red Podcast ↗ greenandredpodcast.org

    A radical podcast fostering critical analysis and mutual aid through in-depth discussions of politics, ecology, and resistance. They coordinate political education, movement coverage, and solidarity networks while building power through anti-capitalist analysis, environmental justice perspectives, and creating spaces for revolutionary dialogue and collective learning.

  4. CounterVortex ↗ countervortex.org

    An independent media platform fostering critical analysis and mutual aid through investigative journalism focused on resistance movements and human rights. They coordinate in-depth coverage of social movements, political analysis, and solidarity networks while building power through radical perspectives, anti-authoritarian reporting, and creating spaces for documenting struggles against oppression worldwide.
