Labor Rights & Worker Support

International Organizations

General Strike US

A mutual aid network organizing worker solidarity and direct support initiatives across the United States. They coordinate resource sharing, strike support, and community organizing while building networks of solidarity through labor organizing and collective action to advance worker power and economic justice.

International Alliance of Waste Pickers

A global network supporting and strengthening waste picker organizations through mutual aid, solidarity, and advocacy. They work to improve working conditions, build collective power, and advance environmental justice while promoting waste pickers' crucial role in the recycling and zero waste economy.
