P.A.I.N. (Prescription Addiction Intervention Now)

Artist-led direct action group targeting the Sackler family and institutions that carry their name, while advocating for harm reduction approaches and fighting stigma around drug use. Founded by Nan Goldin to address the overdose crisis through mutual aid and solidarity.
