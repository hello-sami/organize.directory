Muslim Solidarity

8 groups

National Initiatives

  1. Muslims for Just Futures ↗ muslimsforjustfutures.org

    Muslims for Just Futures (MJF) is a grassroots organization that builds power in Muslim communities through collective care, organizing, advocacy, and movement-building.

  2. Council on American-Islamic Relations (CAIR) ↗ cair.com

    America's largest Muslim civil liberties organization, fostering mutual aid and solidarity through advocacy, education, and community support. They coordinate legal services, anti-Islamophobia campaigns, and community resources while building power through grassroots organizing, policy advocacy, and creating spaces for Muslim Americans to defend their rights and promote justice.

  3. MPower Change ↗ mpowerchange.org

    The largest Muslim-led social and racial justice organization in the United States, building mutual aid and solidarity through grassroots organizing, digital advocacy, and community empowerment. They coordinate campaigns against Islamophobia, surveillance, and discrimination while building power through intersectional movement building, community mobilization, and creating spaces for Muslim political engagement.

  4. Muslim Advocates ↗ muslimadvocates.org

    A national civil rights organization working to halt bigotry in its tracks, fostering mutual aid and solidarity through legal advocacy, policy work, and education. They coordinate legal resources, community safety initiatives, and anti-discrimination campaigns while building power through strategic litigation, policy reform, and creating spaces for Muslims to exercise their rights and participate fully in American life.

  5. Muslim Legal Fund of America (MLFA) ↗ mlfa.org

    A national civil liberties legal fund fostering mutual aid and solidarity through legal advocacy and education. They coordinate legal defense for Muslims facing discrimination and civil liberties violations while building power through strategic litigation, community education, and creating spaces for defending constitutional rights.

  6. ICNA Relief ↗ icna.org

    A Muslim community service organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through direct services to those in need. They coordinate food pantries, disaster relief, refugee services, and shelter homes while building power through community support networks, volunteer mobilization, and creating spaces for addressing poverty and responding to emergencies.

  7. Muslim Anti-Racism Collaborative (MuslimARC) ↗ muslimarc.org

    A racial justice education organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through anti-racism training, resources, and community organizing. They coordinate workshops, learning circles, and advocacy initiatives while building power through intersectional movement building, leadership development, and creating spaces for challenging racism within and beyond Muslim communities.

  8. Muslim Justice League ↗ muslimjusticeleague.org

    A community organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through civil rights advocacy and community education. They coordinate campaigns against surveillance, profiling, and criminalization while building power through grassroots organizing, political education, and creating spaces for challenging state violence against Muslims and other marginalized communities.
