Indigenous Solidarity

Indigenous Environmental Network (IEN)

Alliance of Indigenous Peoples protecting Earth Mother from contamination and exploitation through grassroots organizing, direct action, and building just transition initiatives. Leading campaigns against fossil fuel projects, carbon trading, and false climate solutions while advancing Indigenous knowledge and sovereignty.

Indigenous Anarchist Federation (IAF)

A grassroots federation coordinating mutual aid and solidarity initiatives in support of Indigenous sovereignty and liberation. They organize direct action, resource sharing, and political education while building networks of solidarity through anti-colonial resistance and Indigenous anarchist perspectives.

Native Lives Matter

A grassroots movement fostering mutual aid and community solidarity through advocacy and support for Indigenous communities affected by police violence and systemic injustice. They coordinate direct support initiatives, community education, and solidarity networks while building power through Indigenous-led organizing and creating spaces for healing and collective resistance.

Oglala Lakota Cultural & Economic Revitalization Initiative

Organization that wants to restore resilience, self-sufficiency, economic independence, and revitalize Lakota culture on the Pine Ridge reservation.

Great Plains Action

We are a small society of Indigenous Folks living in the Great Plains region, resisting colonization and re-Indigenizing the land and the world around us.

The Water Protector Legal Collective

An Indigenous-led legal nonprofit that provides support and advocacy for Indigenous peoples and Original Nations, the Earth, and climate justice movements.
