HIV Support

Organizations working to support people living with HIV and prevent new infections through mutual aid and community care.

Regional Organizations

Southern AIDS Coalition

A community-centered organization fostering mutual aid and healthcare access through HIV support and advocacy across the Southern United States. They coordinate capacity building, leadership development, strategic grantmaking, and solidarity networks while building power through policy advocacy, research, and creating spaces for community-driven solutions to end the HIV epidemic in the South. Their work includes direct support services, educational initiatives, and building coalitions to address healthcare disparities in the region.
