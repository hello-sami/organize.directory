Palestinian Solidarity

National Initiatives

Palestinian Youth Movement

Transnational, independent, grassroots movement of young Palestinians organizing for the liberation of Palestine and the return of all Palestinian refugees.

International Solidarity Organizations

International Jewish Anti-Zionist Network (IJAN)

A network of Jews fostering mutual aid and solidarity through anti-colonial and anti-imperialist organizing. They coordinate direct actions, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through Jewish participation in Palestinian liberation struggles and creating spaces for collective resistance against Zionism and oppression.

Rebuilding Alliance

A humanitarian organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through direct support to Palestinian communities. They coordinate rebuilding projects, emergency relief, and advocacy initiatives while building power through grassroots partnerships, policy change campaigns, and creating spaces for Palestinian voices to be heard by Congress and decision-makers.

BDS Movement

A Palestinian-led movement fostering mutual aid and solidarity through boycott, divestment, and sanctions campaigns. They coordinate global grassroots initiatives, economic advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through nonviolent pressure for Palestinian rights and creating spaces for international civil society to support Palestinian liberation.

Decolonize This

A grassroots collective fostering mutual aid and solidarity through Palestinian liberation organizing and decolonial education in the Bay Area. They coordinate direct actions, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through anti-colonial resistance, community organizing, and creating spaces for Palestinian solidarity and liberation.

Refuser Solidarity Network

A network providing support for Israeli war resisters and conscientious objectors through mutual aid and solidarity initiatives. They coordinate legal support, media campaigns, and educational programs while building power through anti-war organizing, resistance to occupation, and creating spaces for Jewish voices against militarism.

Feminist Organizing

Palestinian Feminist Collective

A collective of Palestinian and Arab feminists fostering mutual aid and solidarity through intersectional organizing against gendered, sexual, and colonial violence. They coordinate educational resources, community support, and solidarity networks while building power through feminist approaches to Palestinian liberation and creating spaces for feminist resistance and collective action.

Right of Return

Al-Awda: The Palestine Right to Return Coalition

A grassroots organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through Palestinian right of return advocacy and community organizing. They coordinate direct support initiatives, educational programs, and solidarity networks while building power through Palestinian diaspora organizing, anti-colonial resistance, and creating spaces for Palestinian cultural preservation and collective liberation.

BADIL Resource Center

An independent human rights organization fostering mutual aid and solidarity through research, advocacy, and community support focused on Palestinian refugee and residency rights. They coordinate legal resources, policy research, and solidarity networks while building power through rights-based approaches, documentation of forced displacement, and creating spaces for Palestinian refugee advocacy.

Policy & Research

Al-Shabaka: The Palestinian Policy Network

A global network of Palestinian experts fostering mutual aid and solidarity through critical policy analysis and research. They coordinate policy development, knowledge production, and solidarity networks while building power through Palestinian expertise, strategic thinking, and creating spaces for imagining and developing pathways to liberation.
