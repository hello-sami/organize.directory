Tech Worker Solidarity Networks

Mutual aid networks and initiatives focused on building worker power and solidarity in the tech industry through collective organizing and education.

Tech Workers Coalition (TWC)

A democratically structured coalition fostering mutual aid and solidarity through tech worker organizing and education. They coordinate worker support, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through rank-and-file organizing, cross-movement alliances, and creating spaces for inclusive tech industry transformation.
