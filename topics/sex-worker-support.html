Sex Worker Support

3 groups

Organizations and initiatives focused on building solidarity, providing resources, and advancing the rights of sex workers through mutual aid and community care.

Support Networks

  1. Rouge Support Network ↗ rougesupportnetwork.org

    A mutual aid network providing support, resources, and community for sex workers.

  2. Red Canary Song ↗ redcanarysong.net

    A grassroots mutual aid collective of Asian and migrant sex workers organizing direct support, resource sharing, and community care. They build networks of solidarity through advocacy, education, and organizing while providing mutual aid and harm reduction services to their communities.

  3. Sex Worker's Outreach Project (SWOP) Behind Bars ↗ swopbehindbars.org

    SWOP Behind Bars (SBB) is a national grassroots social justice network dedicated to the fundamental human rights of sex workers, victims of trafficking, and their communities with a focus on those currently incarcerated or seeking re-entry.
