Red Canary Song

A grassroots mutual aid collective of Asian and migrant sex workers organizing directsupport, resource sharing, and community care. They build networks of solidarity through advocacy, education, and organizing while providing mutual aid and harm reduction services to their communities.

Sex Worker's Outreach Project (SWOP) Behind Bars

SWOP Behind Bars (SBB) is a national grassroots social justice network dedicated to the fundamental human rights of sex workers, victims of trafficking, and their communities with a focus on those currently incarcerated or seeking re-entry.
