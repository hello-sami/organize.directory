Police Brutality Resources & Support

5 groups

Organizations and resources focused on addressing police violence, supporting affected families, and building community safety alternatives through mutual aid and solidarity.

Legal Support & Documentation

  1. Michael Nida II ↗ michaelnida.com

    A resource dedicated to documenting cases of police brutality and supporting families affected by police violence. Through mutual aid and advocacy, they work to expose police misconduct, provide support for victims' families, and build networks of solidarity for those impacted by state violence.

Community Support & Action

  1. Families United 4 Justice Group ↗ fu4jgroup.website

    A grassroots network of families and communities affected by police violence, coordinating mutual aid and support initiatives. They organize direct support, advocacy, and solidarity networks while building power through collective healing, family support, and creating spaces for justice and accountability.

  2. Families Demanding Justice ↗ familiesdemandingjustice.org

    A national network of families impacted by police brutality demanding justice

  3. Mothers Against Police Brutality ↗ mothersagainstpolicebrutality.org

    A multi-racial, multi-ethnic coalition uniting mothers who have lost children to police violence. Through mutual aid and advocacy, they work to support affected families, reform deadly force policies, and create positive changes in their communities.

  4. Justice for Skinny Blak ↗ facebook.com

    A community-driven initiative focused on seeking justice and raising awareness about police violence. Through mutual aid and advocacy, they work to support families affected by police brutality and promote accountability in law enforcement.
