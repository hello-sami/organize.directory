Resources & Archives

Jewish Zine Archive

A digital archive preserving and sharing radical Jewish zines, publications, and mutual aid resources. They build solidarity through documenting Jewish resistance, anti-fascist organizing, and community care while making historical and contemporary materials accessible for movement building.

Jewish Anti-Zionist Organizations

International Jewish Anti-Zionist Network (IJAN)

A network of Jews fostering mutual aid and solidarity through anti-colonial and anti-imperialist organizing. They coordinate direct actions, political education, and solidarity networks while building power through Jewish participation in liberation struggles, confronting Zionist backlash, and creating spaces for collective resistance against oppression.
