Anti-Caste Discrimination Initiatives

Mutual aid networks and initiatives focused on ending caste apartheid, supporting Dalit communities, and building caste equity through advocacy and solidarity.

Equality Labs

A Dalit civil rights organization working to end caste apartheid through mutual aid, advocacy, and community healing. They combine grassroots organizing with digital security, research, and arts-based approaches to support caste-oppressed communities and build international solidarity for caste equity.
